Primary CTA button lacks visual prominence

为什么是问题Most local searchers want to call immediately rather than fill a form; the phone number should be the most prominent conversion path but it's currently competing for attention with the green form box, causing visitors to miss the faster action

MAJOR dated_blue_header
Saturated blue header feels dated and generic

观察到The header uses a solid bright cyan-blue background (#0099CC approximate) spanning full width with white text and a script-font tagline ('Family crafted, family cared') — this color scheme and full-bleed approach was common in 2012-2015 WordPress themes

为什么是问题The saturated blue makes the site look like an outdated template rather than a modern local business; younger homeowners (30-45, key renovation demographic) associate this style with sites that haven't been updated in years, suggesting the business may not be current or tech-savvy

正确长啥样Clean white or light neutral header with logo left-aligned, simple sans-serif navigation, and phone CTA right-aligned; single accent color (e.g. coral, deep teal) used sparingly for CTA only; no full-bleed colored backgrounds in header

redesign 怎么改Replace colored header bar with white/off-white background, relocate tagline to hero subheading, simplify navigation to 4-5 clear labels, make phone number a colored button instead of plain text

MINOR cluttered_icon_section
Three-column icon section creates visual clutter

观察到Below the hero are three blue boxes with checkmarks and dense paragraph text ('Same people we all share...', 'Quick & free quotes...', 'Free no phone quotes...') — the text is small, left-aligned, and difficult to scan quickly

为什么是问题Visitors in the awareness stage scan rather than read; three dense paragraphs side-by-side with similar styling cause cognitive overload, making it unclear what the key differentiators are or why they should choose this business

正确长啥样Single-column trust section with 3-4 short bullet benefits (e.g. '30+ Years Brisbane Roofing', 'Free Quote Within 24hrs', 'Fully Licensed & Insured') using large, scannable text with supporting icons; each benefit one line maximum

redesign 怎么改Consolidate into single-column benefit list below hero, use 18px+ text for benefit headlines, eliminate paragraph descriptions or move to separate 'Why Choose Us' section lower on page

MINOR lime_green_form
Bright lime-green form box clashes with brand colors

观察到The quote form on the right uses a bright lime-green background (#AADD00 approximate) with red asterisks for required fields — this green doesn't appear anywhere else on the page and clashes with the blue header

为什么是问题The jarring color contrast makes the form feel like a third-party widget rather than part of the brand; the aggressive lime green can feel pushy or spammy to visitors who aren't ready to commit, potentially triggering a defensive 'not now' reaction

正确长啥样Form with white or very light background, subtle border, single accent color for submit button that matches primary brand color; required field indicators in gray text rather than red asterisks; professional typography hierarchy
