设计观感的整体诊断

CRITICAL mobile_phone_not_actionable
Mobile phone number is not a clear action

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the phone number appears as grey centered text under the logo and wraps onto two lines: “Call Us Today: 07 3062 8404”.

为什么是问题A Google-searching customer on their phone wants to call quickly. When the phone number looks like plain text instead of a large tap-to-call button, some visitors will hesitate, miss it, or choose another roofer with an easier contact path.

正确长啥样A sticky mobile header with two clear buttons: a blue “Call 07 3062 8404” button and an orange “Get Quote” button, both large enough to tap with one thumb.

redesign 怎么改Replace the grey phone text on mobile with a full-width tap-to-call button directly below the logo or in a sticky bottom bar; keep the quote button beside it or directly underneath.

MAJOR dated_mobile_navigation
Old-style mobile menu lowers confidence

观察到The mobile screenshot shows a thin grey navigation bar with “Go to...” on the left and a pale hamburger icon boxed on the right.

为什么是问题This looks like an older website template rather than a current trade business. A homeowner comparing roofers may read that as a sign the company has not updated its online presence or may be less professional.

正确长啥样A simple mobile header with the logo, phone button, quote button, and a clean hamburger icon; no “Go to...” placeholder text.

redesign 怎么改Remove the old select-style navigation and build a modern mobile menu with clear links: Services, Roof Restoration, Roof Repairs, Reviews, Gallery, Contact.

MAJOR desktop_social_share_rail
Social share bar distracts from calling

观察到On the desktop screenshot, a vertical social sharing rail sits on the far left with “48 Shares” and stacked Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Pinterest, Reddit, LinkedIn and other icons.

为什么是问题A homeowner looking for a roofer is not trying to share the page; they are trying to check credibility and make contact. The floating icons add clutter and make the site feel like an old blog rather than a serious local service provider.

正确长啥样No floating share rail; the left edge should be clean, with attention directed to the phone number, service promise, reviews, and quote form.

redesign 怎么改Remove the social sharing rail entirely and use that visual attention for local trust signals such as star rating, suburbs served, years in business, licence or warranty messaging.

MAJOR generic_hero_and_banner
Hero area feels generic

观察到The desktop hero uses a pale roof-and-sky image with a large dark blue banner reading “FREE ADVICE & QUOTES” and the phone number in oversized white type.

为什么是问题The image does not show completed local work, people, vehicles, or proof that this is an active Brisbane roofing company. It communicates contact information, but not enough trust for a homeowner about to spend serious money.

正确长啥样A real Brisbane roof restoration photo or crew-on-site photo, with a short promise, visible phone button, quote button, and one proof line such as rating, warranty, or years of experience.

redesign 怎么改Replace the generic roof-sky banner with a real project photo; overlay a concise headline such as “Roof Restoration Brisbane” plus two CTAs and one concrete trust proof.

MAJOR quote_form_below_action_point
Quote form lacks visible completion

观察到On the desktop screenshot, the right-side orange “Request a Quote” form shows input fields, but the submit button is not visible above the fold.

为什么是问题Visitors need to understand immediately that the form is quick and easy. If they only see a stack of fields with no visible finish point, the form feels longer and more effortful.

正确长啥样A compact quote panel with 3 to 4 fields and a visible high-contrast submit button above the fold, such as “Get My Free Quote”.

redesign 怎么改Shorten the above-fold form to name, phone, suburb, and job type; place a full-width blue or white submit button visible without scrolling.

MAJOR weak_above_fold_trust_signals
Few trust signals above the fold

观察到The visible desktop and mobile areas show logo, phone number, menu, headline, promotion text and quote form, but no visible star rating, customer review snippet, warranty, licence, insurance, or project gallery.

为什么是问题A local-search visitor is comparing multiple roofers and needs fast reassurance that this company is real and reliable. Without visible proof, the page depends mostly on claims.

正确长啥样Above the fold should show at least two trust signals: Google rating or review count, years serving Brisbane, warranty wording, insurance/licence badge, or before-and-after project thumbnails.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust strip directly under the hero or beside the CTA with “Brisbane owned”, “Warranty backed”, “Google rating”, and “Before & after results” using concise labels and real supporting detail.
