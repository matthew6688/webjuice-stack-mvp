CRITICAL placeholder_company_headline
Hero headline looks unfinished

观察到The main hero headline says "YOUR COMPANY FOR ROOF REPAIRS BRISBANE" instead of the visible business name.

为什么是问题A local customer arriving from Google wants to know they found a real Brisbane roofer, and a placeholder-style phrase makes the site feel like a template rather than an established business.

正确长啥样Hero headline uses the actual business name or a direct local offer, such as "Brisbane Roof Repairers" with a short supporting line about leak repairs, restorations, and inspections.

redesign 怎么改Replace the placeholder-style H1 with the real brand/service promise: "Brisbane Roof Repairers" or "Roof Repairs Brisbane - Fast Local Repairs", then add one concrete subline about response area and services.

MAJOR brand_name_missing_from_header
Logo has no business name

观察到The desktop header shows only a white roof icon, and the mobile header shows only a roof icon with colored accents; no business name is visible beside it.

为什么是问题A customer comparing roofers may not remember which company they clicked, and a symbol-only logo gives less confidence than a clear named local business.

正确长啥样Header shows the roof mark plus "Brisbane Roof Repairers" in readable text, with the phone number or call action close by.

redesign 怎么改Add the business name as text next to the logo on desktop and under or beside the mark on mobile, keeping it visible above the fold.

CRITICAL mobile_phone_not_explicit
Mobile call action is unclear

观察到On mobile, the top-left blue square only shows a phone icon, while the actual number is not visible in the header.

为什么是问题Phone shoppers often want to call immediately, but an icon without the number or clear label forces them to guess where to tap and gives less confidence before calling.

正确长啥样Mobile header has a sticky button reading "Call 3608 1803" or a full-width bottom call bar with the phone number visible.

MAJOR trust_proof_absent_above_fold
No proof before asking for contact

观察到The visible hero area contains the logo, headline, service sentence, "Operated by Prostar Roofing", and two buttons, but no reviews, licence, years in business, suburbs served, or guarantee is visible.

为什么是问题Roof repairs are high-trust work; before calling, customers want evidence that the business is legitimate, local, and safe to let onto their property.

正确长啥样Above the fold includes compact proof such as "4.8-star Google rating", "Licensed & insured", "Brisbane-wide roof repairs", and "Leak inspections available".

redesign 怎么改Add a proof strip directly under the hero copy with 3-4 trust badges: Google rating, licensed/insured, Brisbane service area, and repair warranty or inspection offer.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR cta_hierarchy_confusing
Contact buttons compete

观察到The hero shows two similar pill buttons side by side: a blue "CONTACT US" button and an outlined "CALL US TODAY" button.

为什么是问题A local emergency-style visitor should not have to decide between two similar actions; the call action should be the obvious first choice for a roofer.

正确长啥样One dominant phone CTA, such as "Call 3608 1803", with a secondary smaller "Request quote" link.

redesign 怎么改Make the primary CTA a high-contrast tap-to-call button with the number included, and demote the contact form action to a secondary text link or smaller button.

MAJOR mobile_headline_overcrowded
Mobile hero feels cramped

观察到On mobile, the oversized all-caps headline breaks into large stacked lines and fills most of the screen before the service text and buttons.

为什么是问题A phone user has to work harder to scan the message, and the large headline pushes practical details like phone number, reviews, and service proof out of view.

正确长啥样Mobile hero uses a shorter 2-3 line headline, 16px+ readable body text, and a visible call button within the first screen.

redesign 怎么改Rewrite the mobile H1 to a shorter line, reduce the font size and line height, and move the call button plus one trust badge higher in the first viewport.
