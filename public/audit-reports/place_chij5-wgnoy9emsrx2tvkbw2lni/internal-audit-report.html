CRITICAL empty_blue_hero_area
Blank hero area wastes first screen

观察到The main area below the header is a large solid blue block with no headline, roofing photo, offer, suburb mention, review proof, or visible contact details.

为什么是问题A Google-searching Brisbane roofing customer lands here expecting to quickly confirm they found a real roofer who can help now. Seeing a mostly blank first screen makes the site feel unfinished and gives them no reason to keep reading or call.

正确长啥样Above the fold should show a real roof/worksite image or clean service background, a clear headline like 'Roof Repairs & Restorations in Brisbane', a short trust line, star rating or years-in-business proof, and a phone/book button visible immediately.

redesign 怎么改Replace the solid blue hero block with a photo-led hero section containing a Brisbane roofing headline, 1-2 service benefits, Google review proof, and two CTAs: 'Call Now' and 'Request Free Quote'.

CRITICAL mobile_contact_hidden
Mobile has no visible call action

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the header only shows the logo on the left and a hamburger menu on the right; the 'Book a Free Consultation' button and phone action are not visible.

为什么是问题Someone searching for a roofer on their phone often wants to call quickly. If they must open a menu or scroll before finding contact options, many will abandon the site and tap another roofing result.

正确长啥样Mobile header should keep a clear tap-to-call button visible, such as a phone icon plus 'Call', with a secondary menu icon. The phone action should be reachable without opening navigation.

redesign 怎么改Add a sticky mobile header or bottom bar with 'Call Now' and 'Get Quote' buttons, while keeping the hamburger menu as a secondary control.

MAJOR missing_above_fold_trust
No trust proof visible

观察到The visible desktop and mobile areas do not show Google rating, customer reviews, licence/insurance claims, completed work photos, years of experience, or Brisbane service proof.

为什么是问题Roofing is expensive and high-risk for homeowners. Without immediate proof, a visitor cannot tell whether the company is established, local, insured, or worth inviting to inspect their roof.

正确长啥样The first screen should include a compact trust row such as '4.9 Google rating', 'Licensed & insured', 'Brisbane-wide', and '10+ years roofing experience', supported by real project imagery.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust strip directly under the hero headline with review rating, service area, licence/insurance proof, and a small set of real roof project photos or badges.

MAJOR desktop_cta_not_phone_first
Desktop CTA is too vague

观察到The desktop header has a dark blue rounded button saying 'Book a Free Consultation' with a small phone icon, but no phone number is visible.

为什么是问题A local roofing visitor may want to speak to someone immediately. 'Book a Free Consultation' feels slower and less direct than a visible Brisbane phone number or 'Call Now' action.

正确长啥样Desktop header should show the phone number plainly, paired with a strong button such as 'Call 07...' or 'Get Free Roof Quote', with enough contrast and spacing.

redesign 怎么改Change the header CTA to display the actual phone number and add a separate quote button; use one primary accent color and keep the action visible across the site.

MAJOR tiny_low_impact_logo
Logo looks small and dated

观察到The AUS Topline Roofing logo appears small in the top-left, with a dark roof graphic and compressed text that is hard to read, especially in the mobile screenshot.

为什么是问题A small, hard-to-read logo makes the business feel less polished and less established. On mobile, the name is difficult to confirm at a glance.

正确长啥样The header should use a cleaner, sharper logo treatment with readable business name text, consistent spacing, and enough size to identify the company without dominating the page.

redesign 怎么改Rebuild the header brand lockup with a crisp logo file, larger readable wordmark, and optional short descriptor like 'Brisbane Roofing Specialists'.

MINOR navigation_distracts_from_booking
Navigation has distracting links

观察到The desktop navigation includes 'Blogs' and 'Podcasts' alongside core links like Home, About Us, Services, and Contact Us.

为什么是问题A customer looking for roofing help wants services, proof, areas served, and contact options. Podcast and blog links can distract from the main action and make the business feel less focused on solving the immediate roofing problem.

正确长啥样Navigation should prioritize 'Roof Repairs', 'Roof Restoration', 'Gutter Services', 'Projects/Reviews', 'Service Areas', and 'Contact', with the primary quote/call action separate.

redesign 怎么改Simplify the main nav to service and trust pages; move blog or podcast content into the footer or resources section if still needed.

MAJOR below_fold_content_starts_too_late
Useful content starts too low

观察到On desktop, the 'Where We Serve' heading is only barely visible at the very bottom after a large blue block and a large white gap.

为什么是问题Brisbane roofing customers need to know quickly whether their suburb is covered. Hiding service-area information below excessive empty space forces them to work harder before they can trust the business is relevant.

正确长啥样The first screen should hint at the next useful section, with service areas or core services visible just below the hero and no oversized blank vertical gaps.

redesign 怎么改Reduce the hero height, remove the blank white spacer, and bring a compact 'Brisbane suburbs we serve' or 'Roofing services' section into view immediately below the hero.
