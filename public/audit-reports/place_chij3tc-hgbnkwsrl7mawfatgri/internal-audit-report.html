设计观感的整体诊断

The site has clear roofing imagery and visible contact options, but the hero area feels crowded and the quote form is too heavy for a local customer who wants fast reassurance.

MAJOR crowded_hero_message_blocks
Hero message feels crowded

观察到The desktop hero uses two large orange translucent text boxes over the roof photo, with oversized white text taking up much of the left side.

为什么是问题A Google-searching Brisbane customer wants to quickly know whether this roofer is credible and easy to contact. The stacked blocks compete with the photo and form, so the page feels loud instead of reassuring.

正确长啥样One clear headline, one short supporting line, and one primary phone or quote button over a darker photo overlay, with enough spacing around each element.

redesign 怎么改Replace the two orange text panels with a single left-aligned hero message: headline, short proof line such as 'Metal roof replacements across Brisbane', and a prominent call button.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR quote_form_too_large_above_fold
Quote form asks too much too soon

观察到On desktop, the right-side quote form asks for name, email, phone, job address, description, and file upload immediately above the fold.

为什么是问题Someone comparing roofers may not be ready to fill out a long form before they trust the business. The number of fields can make the job feel like effort, especially before seeing proof of workmanship or service guarantees.

正确长啥样A shorter first-step form with name, phone, suburb, and job type, plus a secondary option to upload photos later.

redesign 怎么改Turn the quote panel into a compact lead form: phone number, suburb, service dropdown, and 'Request callback' button; move description and file upload to step two or below the first screen.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

CRITICAL mobile_form_not_actionable_above_fold
Mobile quote form is mostly hidden

观察到In the mobile screenshot, only the 'Request a Free Quote' heading is visible near the bottom of the screen, while the actual form fields are below the fold.

为什么是问题Most local roofing searches happen on phones. If the visitor sees a form title but no field or submit button, they have to keep scrolling before they can act, which adds friction at the exact moment they may be ready to enquire.

正确长啥样On mobile, the first screen should show either a tap-to-call button and one short enquiry field, or a sticky bottom bar with Call and Quote actions.

redesign 怎么改Add a sticky mobile action bar with 'Call' and 'Get Quote' buttons, and place the first form field directly under the quote heading without excess vertical spacing.

证据 · 移动端截图

MAJOR trust_proof_too_thin
Trust proof feels thin

观察到The header shows a Google rating of 5.0 based on 10 reviews, but there are no visible customer photos, project examples, licence details, or service guarantees in the first screen.

为什么是问题Roof work is expensive and risky for homeowners. A 5-star rating helps, but only 10 reviews may not be enough by itself to convince someone before they share their address or request a quote.

正确长啥样Above the fold should include 2-3 concrete trust signals such as licensed and insured, Brisbane-based, before-and-after roof photos, warranty wording, or recent suburb work.

redesign 怎么改Add a compact proof strip below the hero message or form: 'Licensed & insured', 'Brisbane metal roofing specialists', 'Workmanship warranty', and one small project photo thumbnail.

证据截图 · Full desktop

MINOR navigation_visual_weight
Navigation looks heavy

观察到The desktop navigation uses bold black uppercase labels across the full width, while the orange active 'HOME' label and orange call button compete for attention.

为什么是问题The page asks visitors to process too many strong visual signals at once. A local customer should be guided toward calling or requesting a quote, not scanning a heavy menu first.

正确长啥样A quieter navigation row with regular-weight labels, one highlighted phone button, and fewer competing orange elements.

redesign 怎么改Reduce menu font weight, keep only the primary call button in orange, and use a subtle underline or background for the active page state.

证据截图 · Element: header, nav

MINOR file_upload_visual_dominance
Upload area dominates form

观察到The desktop quote form ends with a large pale blue file-upload box and a bright blue 'Choose Files...' button that visually stands out more than some required fields.

为什么是问题Uploading roof photos may be useful, but making it so prominent can make the quote process feel complicated. Some visitors may think they need photos ready before they can enquire.

正确长啥样Photo upload should be optional and visually secondary, with a small link or compact upload control after the main contact fields.

redesign 怎么改Shrink the upload component, label it 'Optional roof photos', and place it after the primary submit button or in the second step of the quote flow.
