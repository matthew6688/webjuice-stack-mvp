MAJOR dated_shadowed_hero_text
Heavy text shadow on hero makes it feel 2012-era

观察到The white hero text 'Brisbane Roof Restoration Specialists' has a thick dark shadow/stroke that creates a heavy outline effect, particularly visible around each letter

为什么是问题Text shadows this prominent immediately date the design to 2008-2014 web aesthetics; modern local-search customers unconsciously associate this visual style with outdated or neglected businesses, reducing perceived credibility before reading any content

正确长啥样Clean white or dark text on a semi-transparent overlay (rgba background) without stroke effects, or a solid color band behind the text with no shadow at all — similar to how Google Business Profile layouts handle text over images

redesign 怎么改Remove all text-shadow CSS from h1 hero text; if contrast is insufficient, add a 60-70% opacity dark overlay to the hero image section rather than shadowing individual letters

MAJOR desktop_form_feels_cramped
Quote form has 13+ checkboxes crammed into hero space

观察到The 'Get a FREE Quote Today!' form on the right shows two text input fields at top, then a dense grid of 13+ small checkboxes for services (Metal Roof, Tile Roof, Roof Repair, etc.) with tiny text labels, making the form feel visually cluttered and overwhelming

为什么是问题A local customer searching for roof restoration wants to quickly request a quote, but this form demands they process 13 service options before even submitting — creating decision fatigue and abandonment. The visual density signals 'work' rather than 'quick help,' especially compared to competitors with simple name/phone/message forms

正确长啥样A simple 3-4 field form above the fold: Name, Phone, Service Type (single dropdown or optional), and a large 'Get Quote' button — no checkboxes. Optionally add 'or call (07) 3152 1900' directly below for phone-first customers

redesign 怎么改Replace checkbox grid with a single optional dropdown for service type (pre-selected to 'General Roof Restoration') or remove service selection entirely and let the business qualify during the phone call; prioritize fast submission over data collection

Red 'Call (07) 3152 1900' button is small and lower contrast than form

观察到The bright red 'Call (07) 3152 1900' button sits below the hero text on the left side, but it's relatively small compared to the large purple 'Get a FREE Quote Today!' form on the right, and the red-on-dark-overlay contrast is lower than the white form background

为什么是问题Many local-search customers (especially older homeowners or time-pressed property managers) prefer to call immediately rather than fill a form; if the phone CTA is visually secondary to a complex form, call-preferred customers may miss it or assume the business prioritizes web leads over phone contact

正确长啥样The phone button should be equal or greater visual weight than the form: larger size (min 48px height for mobile tap), higher contrast (white or bright yellow on dark), and positioned prominently — ideally both a click-to-call button AND the number displayed as text
