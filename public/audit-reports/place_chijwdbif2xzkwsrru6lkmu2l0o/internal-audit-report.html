CRITICAL hero_text_contrast
Low contrast white text on light background

观察到The main headline 'Brisbane Roof Restoration Specialists' is white text overlaid on a light blue sky background. The text has a drop shadow, but the contrast is low, making it look washed out.

为什么是问题A visitor scanning the page on a bright phone screen will struggle to read the headline instantly. If they can't read it easily, they assume the business is unprofessional or the site is broken.

正确长啥样Dark charcoal or navy text on the light background, or a dark semi-transparent overlay behind the text to ensure high contrast.

redesign 怎么改Change headline color to #1a1a1a (dark grey) or add a dark overlay (opacity 0.4) behind the text block.

MAJOR form_visual_clutter
Overwhelming form layout with radio buttons

观察到The desktop form is a dense grid of inputs, followed by four distinct sections of radio buttons (Roof type, Scope, Work Type, Roof age) with yellow labels.

为什么是问题This looks like a data entry task rather than a conversation. A local customer just wants a quote; asking them to categorize their roof type and age before speaking to a human creates friction and anxiety.

正确长啥样A simplified 'Get a Quote' form with only Name, Phone, and Suburb. The technical details should be handled by the sales team later.

redesign 怎么改Remove the radio button sections. Replace with a single 'Tell us about your roof' text area or remove the form entirely in favor of a 'Call Now' button.

证据截图 · Element: form

MAJOR mobile_menu_icon
Hidden navigation on mobile

观察到The mobile screenshot shows a hamburger menu icon in the top right, hiding all navigation links.

为什么是问题Local searchers often look for specific services (e.g., 'roof cleaning' vs 'restoration') or social proof (reviews). Hiding this behind a click adds friction and hides trust signals.

正确长啥样A sticky bottom bar or a visible top bar with the most critical link (e.g., 'Services' or 'Reviews') visible without clicking.

redesign 怎么改Implement a sticky bottom navigation bar on mobile with 'Call', 'Services', and 'Reviews' buttons permanently visible.

MINOR logo_design
Amateurish logo styling

观察到The logo features a hand-drawn style house outline with a red underline and mixed font weights.

为什么是问题Roof restoration is a high-ticket service. A logo that looks like a quick sketch suggests a 'one-man band' rather than an established company, reducing trust for larger jobs.

正确长啥样A clean, vector-based logo with a solid icon and professional typography.

redesign 怎么改Redesign logo to use a solid, geometric roof icon and a clean sans-serif font.
