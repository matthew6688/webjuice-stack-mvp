MAJOR hero_gradient
Outdated Hero Gradient

观察到The large blue gradient background in the hero section appears dated and unprofessional.

为什么是问题Gradients like this were popular in the early 2000s and now make the business look less current and trustworthy. A potential customer searching for a roof restoration service might assume the business isn't up-to-date with modern techniques.

正确长啥样A clean, flat color background or a high-quality, professional photograph of a restored roof.

redesign 怎么改Replace the gradient with a solid, modern color or a professional hero image. Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background.

CRITICAL form_length
Excessive Form Length

观察到The quote request form is very long, requiring multiple fields including 'Roof Age' and a detailed description.

为什么是问题A long form creates friction and discourages potential customers from requesting a quote. People searching locally want quick solutions, not lengthy forms. They may abandon the site and choose a competitor with a simpler process.

正确长啥样A simplified form with only essential fields: Name, Phone, Email, and a brief 'Describe your project' field. Consider progressive disclosure for additional details.

redesign 怎么改Reduce the form to the minimum required information. Implement a 'Get Started' button that leads to a phone call as the primary conversion action.

CRITICAL mobile_form_visibility
Form Obscures Content on Mobile

观察到The form takes up a significant portion of the screen, pushing the main content (image and text) down and potentially requiring scrolling to see it.

为什么是问题On mobile, users expect immediate access to information. Hiding content below the fold without a clear call to action reduces engagement and trust. A user might think the site is poorly designed or untrustworthy.

正确长啥样A single-column layout on mobile with the form positioned below the hero image and key information. Ensure the most important content is visible without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Implement a responsive design that prioritizes content visibility on mobile devices. Stack elements vertically and reduce form length.

MINOR phone_number_repetition
Repeated Phone Number

观察到The phone number (07) 3132 1605 is displayed twice: in the header and as a call to action button.

为什么是问题While making the phone number visible is good, repetition can feel cluttered and slightly unprofessional. It doesn't add significant value.

正确长啥样A single, prominent phone number in the header or a dedicated 'Call Now' button.

redesign 怎么改Remove one instance of the phone number to reduce visual clutter.

MAJOR roof_image_quality
Low-Quality Roof Image

观察到The image of the house with the roof appears slightly blurry and low resolution.

为什么是问题A low-quality image conveys a lack of attention to detail and professionalism. Potential customers may question the quality of the work if the website itself appears unprofessional.
