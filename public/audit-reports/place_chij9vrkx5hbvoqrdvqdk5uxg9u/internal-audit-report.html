设计观感的整体诊断

The site leads with strong 24/7 emergency messaging and a prominent phone number, but a 5-field lead form, a hidden mobile submit button, vague CTA copy, and absent social proof collectively prevent local-search visitors from taking action.

CRITICAL mobile_cta_below_fold
Form submit button invisible on mobile without scrolling

观察到On mobile, the 'Get a FREE Quote' form displays Name, Phone, Email, and Subject fields that consume the full screen height; the 'Message' field and the submit button are cut off below the bottom edge of the viewport at initial load.

为什么是问题A local customer with a burst pipe or blocked drain is in distress and expects an immediate action button on load. If the next step requires scrolling past multiple form fields they haven't filled in yet, most people abandon the page and go back to Google to call a competitor who makes it easier.

正确长啥样A full-width 'Call Now — 0468 515 933' tap-to-call button (minimum 52px height) pinned above the fold as the primary CTA, with a secondary 'Get a Free Quote' link that reveals a shortened 2-field form below it. The submit button must be visible without any scrolling.

redesign 怎么改On mobile: pin a sticky 'Call Now' button to the bottom of the viewport. Replace the 5-field inline form with a 2-field version (Name + Phone only) that fits entirely on screen with the submit button visible. Move Email, Subject, and Message to a non-urgent enquiry page.

证据 · 移动端截图

CRITICAL no_social_proof_above_fold
Zero reviews, ratings, or trust badges in hero section

观察到The entire visible hero area on both desktop and mobile contains only the headline, the dashed '$50 off' button, the lead form, and a starburst badge. No Google star rating, review count, years in business, or plumber's licence number appears anywhere in the above-fold area.

为什么是问题A visitor who arrived from a Google Maps search has already seen a star rating on the listing. When they land on the site and find no confirmation that other customers have had good experiences, they have to take the business's claims on faith. Most don't — they go back to a listing that shows proof.

正确长啥样A one-line trust strip directly below the H1 reading: ★★★★★ 4.8 · 120+ Google Reviews | Licensed & Insured | 15+ Years in Sydney. Small text (12–13px) in a contrasting colour is sufficient; it does not need to be large.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust bar between the headline and the form on both desktop and mobile. Pull the real Google review count and average rating from the GBP listing. Include a Licensed Plumber badge and the number of years in business. Render it in one horizontal line on desktop, two stacked lines on mobile.

MAJOR vague_cta_label
Form submit button reads 'GET A JOB DONE' — unclear intent

观察到The pink submit button at the bottom of the lead form displays the label 'GET A JOB DONE' in uppercase white text.

为什么是问题A visitor filling out a plumbing quote form expects the button to confirm what happens next — 'Send My Request', 'Book a Plumber', or 'Get My Free Quote'. 'Get a Job Done' sounds like a project-management app, not a service booking. Ambiguous button labels create a moment of hesitation that reduces form completions.

正确长啥样A button label that mirrors the visitor's intent: 'Book My Plumber Now', 'Send My Quote Request', or 'Get My Free Quote'. One clear verb, under 5 words, same hot-pink colour for brand consistency.

redesign 怎么改Rename the submit button to 'Book My Plumber Now'. No other styling change required — the colour and size are fine.

MAJOR five_field_form_too_much_friction
5-field lead form creates excessive friction for emergency intent

观察到The 'Get a FREE Quote' hero form has five sequential input fields: Name, Phone, Email, Subject, and Message — all required before reaching the submit button.

为什么是问题A customer with a burst pipe is not in the mindset to write a message and provide a subject line. Each additional field is a reason to give up. Emergency-intent visitors want to provide a phone number and be called back; they are not filling out a detailed enquiry.

正确长啥样A two-field form — Name and Phone — with a visible 'Book My Plumber Now' button. A note below the form: 'We'll call you within 15 minutes.' Move detailed enquiry fields to a separate non-urgent page form.

redesign 怎么改Strip the hero form to Name + Phone only. Remove Email, Subject, and Message from this form entirely. Add a promise of callback speed ('We'll call within 15 min') beneath the submit button.

MAJOR dashed_button_looks_dated
Dashed-border '$50 off' pill reads as unfinished design

观察到A pill-shaped button with a white dashed border and no fill displays the text '$50 off online bookings' in the left portion of the hero on both desktop and mobile screenshots.

为什么是问题Dashed borders on interactive elements were a visual trend circa 2012–2016. Today they read as an unstyled HTML element or a placeholder, not a deliberate design choice. Visitors may not recognize it as clickable, and its dated look signals that the website — and by extension the business — may not be current or professional.

正确长啥样A solid-fill badge or pill in a secondary brand colour (e.g., white text on a semi-transparent dark background, or a yellow/amber badge) with clean typography. Positioned as a supporting discount callout, not a button. No dashed border.

redesign 怎么改Replace the dashed-border pill with a flat solid badge (white background, dark text, or amber) reading '$50 Off — Online Bookings'. Position it as a small tag below the headline, not as a primary interactive element.

MINOR starburst_badge_looks_spammy
Pink starburst badge undermines professional credibility

观察到A hot-pink starburst/sunburst graphic overlaps the top-right corner of the lead form on desktop, and the bottom-right of the hero on mobile, reading 'We're available 24 HRS 7 DAYS' in small white text.

为什么是问题Starburst badges are strongly associated with discount flyers, infomercials, and clearance promotions. On a professional trade services website they signal 'promotional' rather than 'credible and established', which is the opposite of what a distressed customer looking for a reliable plumber needs to feel.

正确长啥样The same information expressed as a clean pill badge with a clock icon, or incorporated into the headline as it already is ('24/7 Emergency'). A simple rounded chip with '⏱ 24/7 Emergency' in white on a dark or teal background would carry the message without the promotional feel.

redesign 怎么改Remove the starburst graphic entirely. The '24/7 Emergency' headline already communicates availability. If a badge is needed, replace it with a small rounded chip: a clock icon + '24/7 Emergency Response' in the trust bar below the headline.

证据截图 · Full desktop

MINOR pink_accent_color_trade_mismatch
Hot-pink brand accent is unusual for a trade services business

观察到The dominant accent colour across the site is a bright hot-pink/magenta, applied to the logo mark, the header 'GET A FREE QUOTE!' button, the form submit button, and the starburst badge.

为什么是问题Hot pink is strongly associated with beauty, fashion, and lifestyle brands. For a plumbing and drainage trade business, it creates a subtle cognitive dissonance — the colour doesn't match the visitor's mental model of a professional trades company — which can erode confidence in the brand's expertise before a word is read.

正确长啥样A bold, energetic accent that still differentiates: deep orange (#FF6B35), a vivid teal (#00B4D8), or electric blue paired with the existing dark background. These colours carry the same energy as hot pink while fitting the trades industry context.

redesign 怎么改A/B test swapping all hot-pink accent instances to a bold orange (#FF6B35). Keep the dark hero background and bold typography unchanged — those are strong. The logo mark may need a minor recolour.
