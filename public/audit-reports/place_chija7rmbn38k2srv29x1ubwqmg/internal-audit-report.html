MAJOR hero_image_quality
Low-Quality Hero Image

观察到The hero image features a stock photo of two people pointing, appearing unnatural and low resolution.

为什么是问题Customers searching for a local service expect professionalism. A low-quality, generic image makes the business appear less credible and trustworthy. It feels like a placeholder, not a real company.

正确长啥样A high-quality, authentic photo of a completed roof renovation project in a local Brisbane setting. Focus on showcasing the quality of work.

redesign 怎么改Replace the stock photo with a professional, high-resolution image of a real roof renovation in the Brisbane area. Prioritize authenticity over staged photos.

MAJOR overused_gradient
Outdated Gradient Effects

观察到The hero section uses a strong blue-to-green gradient, and the logo also incorporates gradients.

为什么是问题Gradients like these were popular in the early 2000s and now make the design look dated and unprofessional. It detracts from the message and makes the business appear less current.

正确长啥样Flat color palettes with subtle shadows or textures. Use a single primary color and a complementary accent color.

redesign 怎么改Remove all gradients and replace them with flat colors. Use a modern color palette that reflects the brand's identity.

CRITICAL mobile_cta_visibility
CTA Below the Fold on Mobile

观察到On mobile, the 'Get in touch for a free quote!' button is located far down the page, requiring significant scrolling.

为什么是问题Mobile users have short attention spans. If the primary call to action isn't immediately visible, they're likely to abandon the site before converting. This is especially critical for local search where users are often looking for immediate contact information.

正确长啥样A prominent, full-width call-to-action button fixed to the bottom of the screen on mobile, or positioned immediately below the hero section.

redesign 怎么改Make the 'Get in touch for a free quote!' button sticky on mobile, or move it directly below the hero image. Ensure it's easily tappable and visible without scrolling.

MAJOR font_legibility
Small Body Text

观察到The body text appears small and difficult to read, especially on desktop.

为什么是问题Small text strains the eyes and makes it difficult for visitors to consume the content. This leads to frustration and a higher bounce rate. People won't read what they can't easily see.

正确长啥样Body text should be at least 16px on desktop and 14px on mobile, with sufficient line height and contrast.

redesign 怎么改Increase the font size of all body text to at least 16px on desktop and 14px on mobile. Improve line height and contrast for better readability.

MINOR social_icon_clarity
Social Icons Lack Hover State

观察到The social media icons do not change state on hover.

为什么是问题Lack of visual feedback on hover makes the icons appear static and less interactive. Users may not realize they are clickable.

正确长啥样Social icons should have a subtle hover state (e.g., color change, slight animation) to indicate they are clickable.

redesign 怎么改Add a hover state to the social media icons to provide visual feedback to users.

需要保留的优点

  • Clear contact information is present in the header.
  • The navigation menu is straightforward and easy to understand.
  • The use of a tagline ('Same house, more home.') is memorable.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Make CTA visible above the fold on mobile
  2. 2. Replace hero image with authentic local photography
  3. 3. Modernize color palette and remove gradients
