设计观感的整体诊断

The site features a clear value proposition and strong imagery, but the navigation is cluttered and the primary call-to-action is buried below the fold, making it difficult for mobile users to initiate contact immediately.

Primary Call-to-Action Buried Below Fold

观察到The main 'Get in touch for a free quote!' button is located at the bottom of the hero section, requiring the user to scroll past the headline and introductory text to see it.

为什么是问题Local search customers on mobile devices often look for a way to act immediately. If they have to scroll to find the button, they may assume the site is hard to use or simply bounce to a competitor with a visible phone number or button.

正确长啥样A 'Get a Free Quote' button or phone number should be visible in the top right corner of the header or immediately next to the main headline without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Move a primary CTA button into the sticky header navigation and place a secondary CTA button directly next to the 'Bigger. Brighter. Better.' headline.

Overcrowded Navigation Menu

观察到The top navigation bar contains 8 distinct text links (Home, About Us, Our Services, Our Product Range, Be Inspired, Our Showroom, FAQs, Contact Us) plus social icons and contact info.

为什么是问题This creates visual noise and cognitive overload. A visitor trying to find 'Roofing' or 'Quotes' has to scan too many options. On mobile, this will likely require a hamburger menu that hides the contact info, hurting conversion.

正确长啥样A simplified menu with 4-5 core items (Home, Services, Gallery, About, Contact) and a distinct, high-contrast button for 'Get a Quote'.

redesign 怎么改Consolidate 'Our Services', 'Product Range', and 'Showroom' into a 'Services' dropdown. Move 'FAQs' to the footer. Make 'Contact Us' a button rather than a text link.

证据截图 · Element: header, nav

MAJOR weak_header_hierarchy
Distracting Top Utility Bar

观察到A dark teal bar at the very top spans the full width, containing the service area text, address, phone number, and social icons.

为什么是问题This bar competes for attention with the logo and navigation. It feels like a 'banner ad' style design pattern that is visually heavy and pushes the actual content down, making the page feel longer than it is.

正确长啥样A clean white or light grey header. The phone number should be a clickable link in the top right, and the service area text should be removed or placed in the footer.

redesign 怎么改Remove the dark teal top bar. Integrate the phone number into the main white header on the right side. Remove the 'Servicing Brisbane...' text from the header to reduce clutter.

证据截图 · Full desktop

MINOR hero_text_legibility
Low Contrast Hero Text

观察到The white headline text 'Bigger. Brighter. Better.' sits on top of a blue-tinted photo of a room. The contrast is moderate but not high.

为什么是问题On bright screens or in sunlight (common for mobile users), the white text may be difficult to read against the light blue background, causing the user to miss the value proposition.

正确长啥样A darker overlay on the hero image or a drop shadow on the text to ensure the white letters pop clearly against the background.

redesign 怎么改Increase the opacity of the blue overlay on the hero image to at least 60% or add a subtle text-shadow to the headline.

需要保留的优点

  • The headline 'Same House, more home!' is catchy and clearly communicates the benefit of the service.
  • The use of a family photo in the hero section creates an emotional connection and illustrates the 'more space' benefit well.
  • The logo is clear and legible, establishing brand identity immediately.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Move the 'Get a Quote' CTA into the header and next to the headline for immediate visibility.
  2. 2. Simplify the navigation menu to reduce cognitive load and highlight the contact button.
  3. 3. Remove the dark top utility bar to create a cleaner, more modern header structure.
