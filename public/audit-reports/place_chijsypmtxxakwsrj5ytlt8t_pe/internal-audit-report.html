CRITICAL mobile_above_fold_no_service_context
Mobile first screen lacks roofing proof

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the first visible area is mostly social icons, a blue 'REQUEST A QUOTE' button, the large PRO logo, phone number, and a grey 'Go to...' navigation bar; no roof photo, completed work, reviews, or service proof is visible before the welcome heading.

为什么是问题A local customer searching for a roofer on their phone wants to quickly confirm this is a real roofing business that can solve their problem. Seeing only branding and a generic welcome section makes them work harder before they trust the company enough to call.

正确长啥样Mobile should open with a compact header showing logo, tap-to-call button, and a hero area with a real roof restoration photo, a short Brisbane-specific promise, star rating or review count, and one clear quote button visible without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Replace the mobile top stack with a 56px sticky header containing logo and phone CTA, then add a first-screen hero with a real completed roof image, headline 'Roof Restoration Brisbane', review/trust line, and two actions: 'Call Now' and 'Get Free Quote'.

Floating share bar distracts

观察到On the desktop screenshot, a vertical social sharing strip sits on the far left edge with '48 Shares' and stacked Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Pinterest, Reddit, LinkedIn, and other icons.

为什么是问题A person looking for a roofer is not trying to share the page; they are trying to decide who to call. The share bar feels like an old blog plugin and visually competes with the actual contact actions.

正确长啥样The page should have no floating share toolbar; trust elements should be focused on reviews, licences, service areas, workmanship guarantees, and contact options.

redesign 怎么改Remove the floating social share plugin entirely and replace that trust space with visible Google rating, years in business, warranty badge, and Brisbane service-area proof near the hero and quote form.

需要保留的优点

  • The business name and phone number are visible near the top on both desktop and mobile.
  • The brand colors are consistent enough to reuse in a more restrained redesign.
  • There is already a quote action and quote form, so the redesign can improve placement rather than inventing a new contact path.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Rebuild the mobile first screen around tap-to-call, quote CTA, real roof imagery, and immediate trust proof.
  2. 2. Replace the dated desktop hero and floating share bar with a high-quality project photo, concise service headline, review proof, and clear actions.
  3. 3. Simplify the quote journey with fewer fields, consistent CTA styling, and visible Google reviews, warranty, and local Brisbane credibility.
