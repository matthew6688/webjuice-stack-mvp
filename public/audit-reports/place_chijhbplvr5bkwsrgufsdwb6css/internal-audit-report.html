视觉审计 · Vision LLM

设计观感的整体诊断

The site looks legitimate and established, but the above-the-fold experience makes it harder than necessary for a Brisbane roofing customer to call or request a quote quickly.

CRITICAL mobile_phone_number_hidden
Mobile phone number is hidden

观察到On the mobile header, there is only a black phone icon beside the logo, with no visible phone number or call text.

为什么是问题A local-search visitor on their phone is often ready to call immediately, especially for roof repairs or quote requests. If they have to guess whether the icon is tappable or open the menu first, some will leave and call another roofer.

正确长啥样A mobile sticky header with a clearly labeled tap-to-call button such as 'Call 07 3506 2534' or a prominent green phone button with supporting text visible without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Replace the lone phone icon with a high-contrast sticky call button showing the number or 'Call Now', and keep it visible at the top of the mobile viewport.

证据 · 移动端截图

MAJOR no_hero_cta_mobile
No quote button above fold on mobile

观察到The mobile screenshot shows the headline, intro copy, review claim, and offer list, but no visible 'Get a Quote' or call-to-action button in the first screen.

为什么是问题The page builds interest but does not give the visitor an obvious next step. Someone searching 'roofer Brisbane' should be able to request help before reading a long list of services.

正确长啥样Below the mobile headline, show two large buttons: 'Call Now' and 'Get a Free Quote', followed by the review proof and service bullets.

redesign 怎么改Move a primary quote button and secondary call button directly under the hero headline on mobile, before the 'What we offer' list.

证据 · 移动端截图

MAJOR desktop_hero_cta_missing
Hero lacks direct action

观察到On desktop, the main hero card contains the large 'Metal Roofing Specialists' headline, review text, and bullet list, but no quote or call button inside the hero card.

为什么是问题The visitor sees a strong service message but must look back up to the navigation bar to act. That creates friction for a person quickly comparing roofing companies.

正确长啥样The hero card should include a large primary 'Get a Free Quote' button and a secondary 'Call (07) 3506 2534' link directly below the trust text.

redesign 怎么改Add two clear hero actions inside the white card: an orange or green quote button and a phone action, placed before the service bullet list.

证据截图 · Desktop 800px region

MAJOR desktop_navigation_too_busy
Navigation feels crowded

观察到The desktop navigation contains multiple dropdown items, Projects, Testimonials, Contact, a large orange 'Get a Quote' button, and a search icon all in one row.

为什么是问题A roofing customer usually needs proof, service fit, and a fast way to call. Too many navigation choices make the page feel more like a directory than a focused local business page.

正确长啥样A simpler header with Services, Projects, Reviews, Contact, plus one dominant 'Get a Quote' button and the phone number.

redesign 怎么改Condense the header navigation into 4 core links, remove or de-emphasize the search icon, and keep quote plus phone as the visual priority.

证据截图 · Element: header, nav

MINOR search_icon_competes_with_quote
Search icon distracts from quote

观察到A black search icon appears at the far right of the desktop header immediately after the orange 'Get a Quote' button.

为什么是问题For a local roofing site, search is less important than calling or requesting a quote. The icon pulls attention away from the main action.

正确长啥样The right side of the header should end with the strongest action only: phone number and quote button, without a competing utility icon.

redesign 怎么改Remove the desktop search icon from the primary header or move search into the footer/resources area if it is still needed.

证据截图 · Element: form

MINOR hero_image_secondary_to_card
Roofing photo is underused

观察到The desktop hero photo of a metal roof sits mostly behind and beside a large white text card, while the mobile screenshot shows no roofing image in the first screen.

为什么是问题Roofing is a trust-heavy service where customers want to see real workmanship quickly. When the image is secondary or absent on mobile, the page relies heavily on claims instead of visual proof.

正确长啥样A crisp project photo visible near the top on both desktop and mobile, with a small caption such as 'Metal roof installation, Brisbane' and trust proof nearby.

redesign 怎么改Use a stronger above-fold project image crop on desktop and add a compact real project image below the mobile CTA before the long service list.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

需要保留的优点

  • The logo is clear and professional in both desktop and mobile headers.
  • The phone number is visible on desktop and paired with a free quote message.
  • The hero copy includes useful trust proof, including '100+ 5 Star Reviews' and a 10-year workmanship guarantee.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Add a visible sticky mobile call/quote action above the fold.
  2. 2. Place primary quote and phone buttons directly inside the hero area.
  3. 3. Simplify the desktop navigation so phone, reviews, projects, and quote are the main path.
