设计观感的整体诊断

The site shows the business name and contact icons clearly, but the heavy dark styling, weak trust proof, and unclear mobile actions make it feel older than a current Brisbane roofing business.

MAJOR dated_dark_roof_texture
Heavy dark background feels dated

观察到Both screenshots use a black roof-tile texture behind the header and content, with white text sitting directly on the dark image.

为什么是问题A Google-searching local customer may read the dark textured layout as old or low-budget, especially when comparing roofers side by side. It makes the business feel less current even if the work is legitimate.

正确长啥样A light, clean page background with dark readable text, one strong orange accent color, and real project photography used in framed sections rather than as a repeated texture.

redesign 怎么改Replace the black texture background with a white or very light grey layout, keep orange for primary actions, and use roof photos only as hero or gallery images.

证据截图 · Full desktop

CRITICAL mobile_no_visible_phone_number
Mobile phone number is hidden

观察到On mobile, the top area shows a round orange phone icon but no visible phone number next to it.

为什么是问题Many local roofing customers want to call quickly, especially for leaks or urgent repairs. If they cannot see the number immediately, some will hesitate or tap back to another roofer.

正确长啥样A sticky mobile header with a visible phone number or a full-width 'Call 0432 254 975' button that stays easy to tap.

redesign 怎么改Add a persistent mobile call bar showing 'Call 0432 254 975' with the phone icon, and keep it visible at the top or bottom of the screen.

证据 · 移动端截图

MAJOR missing_quote_cta
No clear quote action above fold

观察到The desktop header shows a phone number and two round icons, but there is no visible 'Get a Quote' or 'Request Inspection' button in the first screen.

为什么是问题Not every visitor is ready to call. A Brisbane homeowner comparing roofers may prefer to request a quote quietly, and the page does not give them an obvious next step.

正确长啥样A prominent orange button reading 'Get a Free Roof Quote' beside the phone number on desktop and below the hero headline on mobile.

redesign 怎么改Add a primary CTA button above the fold: 'Get a Free Roof Quote', linking to a short contact form, with phone as the secondary action.

MAJOR thin_trust_proof
Trust proof is not visible

观察到The visible first screen shows the logo, phone, menu, hero image, and text, but no Google rating, review count, licence, insurance, warranty, or local suburb proof.

为什么是问题Roof restoration is a high-trust purchase. A homeowner searching 'roofer Brisbane' wants quick proof that the business is real, reviewed, insured, and local before contacting them.

正确长啥样Above-fold trust row with Google star rating, '13+ years experience', licence/insured badge, service area, and a workmanship warranty statement.

redesign 怎么改Place a compact trust strip under the hero or header with review rating, years in business, Brisbane service area, warranty, and licence/insurance details.

MINOR desktop_nav_looks_sparse
Navigation feels sparse

观察到The desktop orange navigation bar contains only 'About', 'Gallery', and 'Contact' spread widely across the full page.

为什么是问题A visitor looking for roof repairs, roof painting, tile replacement, or restoration details cannot immediately see whether the business handles their exact job.

正确长啥样A concise service-led navigation with 'Roof Restoration', 'Roof Repairs', 'Roof Painting', 'Gallery', and 'Contact' plus a quote button.

redesign 怎么改Replace the broad three-item nav with service-specific links and a right-aligned quote CTA on desktop.

MINOR mobile_orange_empty_bar
Mobile has an empty orange strip

观察到On mobile, a solid orange horizontal bar appears below the three round header icons with no text or visible purpose.

为什么是问题An empty colored strip takes valuable mobile space and creates confusion. Local customers on phones need every top-screen element to help them act quickly.

正确长啥样That space should either be removed or turned into a meaningful call-to-action bar such as 'Free Brisbane Roof Quote'.

redesign 怎么改Convert the empty orange strip into a tappable CTA bar or remove it and move the hero content higher.

MAJOR hero_image_lacks_message
Hero image lacks a selling message

观察到The main house photo appears as a wide image with no overlaid headline, service promise, suburb relevance, or call button.

为什么是问题The image shows a nice roof, but it does not immediately tell a new visitor why this roofer is the right choice or what to do next.

正确长啥样Hero section with a real roof restoration image, headline like 'Brisbane Roof Restoration & Repairs', a short trust line, and call/quote buttons visible on top or directly below.
