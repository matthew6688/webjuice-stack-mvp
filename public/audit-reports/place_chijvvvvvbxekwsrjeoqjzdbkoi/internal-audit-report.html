CRITICAL black_background_legibility
Black background makes text unreadable

观察到The entire page uses a pure black background (#000000) with grey or white text that has low contrast. The navigation menu text ('ROOFING SERVICES', 'NEWS AND UPDATES') is barely visible against the black.

为什么是问题Visitors scanning the page on a phone or laptop will struggle to read the menu options. If they can't read the text, they assume the site is broken or the business is inactive, leading them to click back to Google immediately.

正确长啥样A white or very light grey background with dark grey or black text. Navigation items should be clearly legible without squinting.

redesign 怎么改Invert the color scheme: use a white background for the body and navigation, with dark text for maximum readability.

MAJOR outdated_bevel_graphics
1990s-style bevels and gradients

观察到The phone number button ('1300 4 DIAMOND') and the main logo use heavy 3D bevels, drop shadows, and gradients that look like they were designed in 1998.

为什么是问题These visual styles signal that the website has not been updated in 15+ years. A customer looking for a professional roofer will assume the business is either out of touch or no longer in operation.

正确长啥样Flat design elements. The phone button should be a solid color (e.g., blue or orange) with white text, no 3D borders or shadows.

redesign 怎么改Remove all bevels, gradients, and drop shadows. Use flat, solid colors for buttons and logos to look current.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR cluttered_header_layout
Header is visually cluttered

观察到The top section is packed with the logo, four award badges, a license number, and a phone button all crammed into one area without clear hierarchy.

为什么是问题The eye doesn't know where to look first. The phone number, which is the most important element for a local service, is buried among awards and text.

正确长啥样A clean header with the logo on the left and a prominent 'Call Now' button on the right. Awards can be moved to a footer or a dedicated 'Trust' section.

redesign 怎么改Simplify the header. Place the logo on the left, navigation in the center, and a large, high-contrast phone button on the right.

CRITICAL low_contrast_navigation
Navigation text is invisible

观察到The menu items 'ROOFING SERVICES', 'NEWS AND UPDATES', and 'CONTACTS' are in a light grey color on a black background, making them nearly invisible.

为什么是问题Users cannot navigate the site to find specific services. If they can't find 'Roofing Services', they can't convert.

正确长啥样Navigation text should be dark on a light background, or white on a dark background with high contrast.

redesign 怎么改Change navigation text color to white or dark grey depending on the background, ensuring a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1.
