MAJOR mobile_phone_number_hidden
Mobile phone number is not visible

观察到On the mobile header, the contact option is a green circular phone icon only; the number itself is not shown.

为什么是问题A local customer comparing roofers on their phone may want to confirm the number before tapping, especially for a high-value trade job. Hiding the number makes the business feel less immediately reachable.

正确长啥样A sticky mobile header with the logo, a visible phone number or 'Call 0498 478 739' button, and a quote button accessible within one tap.

redesign 怎么改Replace the standalone green phone icon with a compact visible call button reading 'Call 0498 478 739' or add a sticky bottom bar with 'Call' and 'Get Quote'.

证据 · 移动端截图

MAJOR above_fold_missing_trust_proof
No proof shown above the fold

观察到The hero area shows a large roof photo, headline, paragraph, and quote/service buttons, but no review rating, license, warranty, years in business, or completed-project proof is visible.

为什么是问题Roofing is expensive and risky for homeowners and builders. A visitor from Google needs quick proof that the company is legitimate before asking for a quote.

正确长啥样The first screen includes 2-4 compact proof points such as star rating, QBCC/license detail, insured status, warranty, and 'Brisbane-based metal roofing specialists'.

redesign 怎么改Add a proof strip directly under the headline or CTA with icons and short labels: 'QBCC licensed', 'Brisbane owned', 'Metal roof specialists', and review rating if available.

MAJOR headline_targets_builders_not_homeowners
Headline may exclude local homeowners

观察到The main headline says 'Trusted by Queensland's Premium Builders' and the paragraph focuses on builders and developers.

为什么是问题Someone searching 'roofer Brisbane' may be a homeowner with a repair, replacement, or new roof need. The wording can make them think the business only works with builders.

正确长啥样A headline that still signals premium work but names the service and location, such as 'Metal Roofing Specialists in North Brisbane' with subtext for homes, new builds, and commercial projects.

redesign 怎么改Rewrite the hero copy to lead with service and area, then mention builder trust as proof: 'North Brisbane Metal Roofing for New Builds, Re-roofs and Commercial Projects'.

MINOR desktop_cta_competes_with_quote_button
Quote actions are split

观察到On desktop there is a dark 'Quote Request' button in the navigation and a green 'Get a Free Quote' button in the hero.

为什么是问题Two differently styled quote actions can make the page feel less focused. A visitor may not instantly know which button is the main path.

正确长啥样One consistent primary quote style repeated across header and hero, with phone as the secondary action.

redesign 怎么改Use the same green primary CTA styling for both quote buttons, label them consistently as 'Get a Free Quote', and reserve dark styling for secondary navigation.

MINOR mobile_secondary_button_cut_off
Mobile secondary button is cropped

观察到At the bottom of the mobile screenshot, a bordered secondary button is partially hidden by the diagonal white section.

为什么是问题A cropped button makes the page feel unfinished and can frustrate users who try to understand what else they can tap.

正确长啥样Mobile hero buttons stack cleanly with full labels visible and at least 16px spacing before the next section shape.

redesign 怎么改Stack the hero buttons vertically on mobile, remove the diagonal overlap from the button area, and keep both CTA labels fully visible above the fold or move the secondary button below the fold.

MINOR desktop_header_takes_large_vertical_space
Desktop header is oversized

观察到The desktop header uses a tall white navigation area with a large logo block, pushing the hero content lower on the screen.

为什么是问题The first screen has less room for trust proof, service clarity, and contact options, so visitors must work harder to decide whether to act.

正确长啥样A slimmer desktop header around 80-96px tall with logo, nav, phone, and quote action aligned in one clear row.

redesign 怎么改Reduce desktop header height, scale the logo slightly smaller, keep phone and quote CTA prominent, and use the freed space for proof points under the hero copy.
