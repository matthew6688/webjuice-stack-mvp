## Observed Brand Values

- Existing site is built around black and white theme sections, white type logo on dark photography, and square primary buttons.
- Live font source is Montserrat from Google Fonts; use it for body/navigation and pair with a restrained serif display face to elevate the concept without abandoning the current identity.
- Accent usage should be spare: brass/warm metal only for booking action, small section metadata, and thin rules.
- Photography carries the atmosphere. Use official local assets derived from the captured site: `assets/rich-and-rare-hero.jpg`, `assets/rich-and-rare-menu.jpg`, and `assets/rich-and-rare-logo-white.png`.
- Layout posture: full-width cinematic hero, high-contrast dark dining room surfaces, ivory menu panels, square buttons, hairline dividers, minimal radii, no decorative icons.
