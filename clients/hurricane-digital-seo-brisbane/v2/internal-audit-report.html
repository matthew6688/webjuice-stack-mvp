设计观感的整体诊断

The visual design is modern and clean, but the content strategy is confusing for a roofing business, and the primary call-to-action is a low-commitment email form rather than a direct contact method.

CRITICAL industry_mismatch
Content does not match roofing industry

观察到The headline reads 'Transform Your Local Business with Expert Digital Marketing' and the subhead is 'Flood your Business with Partners'.

为什么是问题A customer searching for 'roofing Brisbane' is looking for a roofer, not a marketing agency. This text signals that the website belongs to an SEO company, not a contractor. The visitor will immediately bounce because they cannot find roofing services.

正确长啥样Headlines should focus on the service provided, e.g., 'Brisbane's Trusted Roofing Experts' or 'Quality Roof Repairs & Installations'.

redesign 怎么改Replace all marketing-focused copy with service-focused copy. The hero section must clearly state that this is a roofing company offering repairs, maintenance, or installation.

证据截图 · Full desktop

CRITICAL weak_primary_cta
Primary CTA is an email form, not a phone call

观察到The main call-to-action button is 'Book Free Strategy Session' next to an 'Enter Your Email Here' input field.

为什么是问题Local service customers (especially in emergencies like roof leaks) want immediate answers. They do not want to fill out a form or book a 'strategy session' (which sounds like a sales pitch for marketing). They want to call a phone number or request a quote.

正确长啥样A large, high-contrast button that says 'Get a Free Quote' or 'Call Now' with a clickable phone number (tel: link) visible above the fold.

redesign 怎么改Replace the email input and 'Strategy Session' button with a prominent 'Get a Free Quote' button and a visible phone number in the header.

证据截图 · Desktop 800px region

MAJOR hidden_contact_info
Phone number is not in the main navigation

观察到The top navigation bar contains links (Home, About Us, Services) and buttons (Client Login, Speak to an Expert), but no phone number is visible in the header.

为什么是问题Trust is built on accessibility. If a user cannot see a phone number at the very top of the page, they may doubt the legitimacy of the business or assume it is difficult to reach.

正确长啥样A phone number (e.g., (07) 3132 8715) should be placed in the top right corner of the header, next to the logo or navigation links.

redesign 怎么改Add the phone number to the header navigation, ensuring it is clickable on mobile devices.

证据截图 · Element: header, nav

MINOR generic_hero_image
Hero image shows an office, not a roof

观察到The large image on the right shows a modern office reception desk with the 'Hurricane Digital' logo on the wall.

为什么是问题This reinforces the 'marketing agency' vibe. A roofing customer wants to see roofs, workers, or before/after shots of repairs. An office photo feels corporate and disconnected from the physical work.

正确长啥样A high-quality photo of a completed roofing project, a team member on a roof, or a friendly team photo in front of a work van.

redesign 怎么改Replace the office reception photo with a relevant image of roofing work or the team at a job site.

需要保留的优点

  • The visual design is clean, with good use of whitespace and a professional color palette (blue/white).
  • The typography is modern and legible, with clear hierarchy between headlines and subheads.
  • The 'Speak to an Expert' button in the header is a good secondary call-to-action, though it needs to be more prominent.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Rewrite all copy to focus on roofing services, not digital marketing.
  2. 2. Replace the email form with a 'Get a Free Quote' button and add a clickable phone number to the header.
  3. 3. Replace the office photo with a relevant image of roofing work or the team.
