FAQ

Straight answers to the questions Brisbane homeowners ask.

If your question isn't here, call 0449 168 985 — we'll tell you whether it's worth a site visit.

Is the roof inspection genuinely free?

Yes. We inspect, photograph, and assess the roof condition at no charge. You only pay if you choose to proceed with quoted work. No obligation, no pressure.

Restoration or full replacement — how do I know which I need?

Roof age matters less than structural condition. If the frame, sarking, and tiles are fundamentally sound, restoration usually gives another 10-15 years. Severe rust, soft tiles, or structural damage typically means replacement. The inspection report shows you which applies.

How quickly can you respond to a leak?

Same-week response for urgent repairs. We'll arrange temporary weather protection if needed, then schedule the permanent fix. For storm damage with insurance involvement, we provide detailed photo documentation for your claim.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed roofing specialist, fully insured. We'll provide documentation before starting any work.

Do I need to be home for the inspection?

Helpful but not essential. We can inspect the roof exterior and take photos without access inside. If you're available we'll walk you through the findings on-site; otherwise we'll send the photo report and call to discuss.
