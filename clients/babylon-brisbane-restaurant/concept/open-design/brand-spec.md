## Observed values

- Site CSS declares `--color-background: #fff9f1`, `--color-dark: #572a31`, `--color-mid: #622d51`, `--color-light: #eed383`, plus darker wine borders.
- Typography imports Typekit and uses `transat` for body with `mendl-sans-dawn` for headings.
- Logo asset: `https://babylonbrisbane.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/logo.png`; negative mark appears in hero as `BAB-Brisbane-Negative-1-1024x364.png`.
- Imagery posture is hospitality-first: warm food, terraces, river views, arched top crops, square event cards, generous editorial spacing.

## Layout posture

- Cream background with deep wine typography; gold is a restrained accent for buttons, tags and key dividers.
- Headings are uppercase, light-weight, display-driven and spacious; body copy stays warm and direct.
- Buttons are low radius, outlined or filled, with clear booking/function actions.
- Use arched or rounded-top photography motifs instead of generic cards.
- Navigation intent: Eat & Drink with Restaurant/Gardens sub intent, What’s On, Functions, Contact, Function Enquiry, Book a table.
