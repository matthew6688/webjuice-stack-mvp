MAJOR hero_image_quality
Low-Resolution Hero Image

观察到The hero image on desktop appears pixelated and blurry, especially noticeable in the roofing tiles.

为什么是问题A blurry image immediately signals a lack of professionalism and attention to detail. A potential customer searching for a quality roofing service will likely assume the business doesn't care about quality if their own website looks sloppy.

正确长啥样High-resolution, professionally shot image of a completed roofing project, showcasing quality workmanship. Image should be optimized for web to load quickly.

redesign 怎么改Replace the current hero image with a high-resolution, professionally photographed image. Ensure image is compressed for web performance.

CRITICAL hidden_phone_number
Hidden Phone Number (Mobile)

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the phone number is tucked away in the header and requires scrolling to see.

为什么是问题Mobile users are often searching 'on the go' and need immediate access to contact information. Hiding the phone number creates friction and makes it difficult for them to quickly call for a quote or emergency service, leading to lost leads.

正确长啥样Prominent, 'sticky' phone number at the top of the mobile screen, easily tappable. Consider a 'Call Now' button.

redesign 怎么改Implement a sticky header on mobile with a clearly visible and tappable phone number. Add a 'Call Now' button.

MAJOR dated_visual_style
Outdated Visual Style

观察到The website uses a dated color palette (muted blues and grays) and a generic, stock-photo aesthetic. The overall design feels flat and lacks visual interest.

为什么是问题An outdated design can make the business appear less trustworthy and established. Potential customers may perceive the business as being behind the times and less capable.

正确长啥样Modern color palette (e.g., blues, grays, and a vibrant accent color), high-quality photography of actual projects, and a clean, minimalist layout.

redesign 怎么改Update the color palette and typography to reflect a more modern aesthetic. Replace stock photos with high-quality images of completed projects.

MAJOR lack_of_social_proof
Missing Testimonials/Reviews

观察到There are no visible customer testimonials or reviews on either the desktop or mobile screenshot.

为什么是问题Potential customers rely heavily on social proof when choosing a local service provider. The absence of testimonials creates doubt and makes it harder to build trust.

正确长啥样Dedicated section on the homepage showcasing customer testimonials with photos and/or star ratings. Integration with Google Reviews or other review platforms.

redesign 怎么改Add a section on the homepage to display customer testimonials and reviews. Integrate with Google Reviews.

MINOR generic_cta
Vague Call to Action

观察到The 'Get a Quote' button is generic and doesn't convey a sense of urgency or value.

为什么是问题A weak call to action can discourage visitors from taking the next step. It doesn't clearly communicate the benefits of requesting a quote.

正确长啥样Specific and benefit-driven call to action, such as 'Get a Free Roofing Estimate' or 'Schedule Your Inspection Today'.

redesign 怎么改Rewrite the call to action to be more specific and benefit-driven.

Redesign 优先级
