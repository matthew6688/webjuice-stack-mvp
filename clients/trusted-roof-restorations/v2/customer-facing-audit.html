What's Working Well

  • You've built an outstanding reputation
    128 five-star Google reviews is excellent. Most roofing businesses struggle to get even 20 reviews. This shows customers trust you and are happy to recommend you.
  • Your phone number is visible on desktop

  • Your branding is clear
    Your business name and logo are easy to spot, and the orange colour you use is memorable and professional.

What's Holding You Back

  • Your website shows a "Not Secure" warning
    Every visitor sees a security warning in their browser. Google research shows that over 80% of people close the page immediately when they see this. You're losing roughly 8 out of every 10 potential customers before they even read your page. This also hurts your Google ranking directly.
  • Mobile visitors can't easily see your phone number
    About 7 out of 10 people searching for roofing services are on their phone. Your mobile site shows an orange icon, but no actual phone number next to it. People have to guess whether it's a call button. Many will simply go back to Google and call a competitor whose number is clearly visible.
  • The design looks dated
    The heavy black background and overall style look like a website from 5–10 years ago. When customers are deciding who to trust with a $5,000–$15,000 roofing job, first impressions matter. An outdated website makes people wonder if you're still actively in business.
  • There's no "Get a Quote" button
    Many people browse your website during work hours or in the evening when they can't call. Without a quote form, you're losing these leads to competitors who make it easy to request a quote online. You can only capture people who are ready to call right now.
  • Your trust signals are buried
    Your 5-star rating, licence, insurance, and warranty aren't visible when someone first lands on your page. Roofing is expensive and risky—people need to feel confident immediately. Right now, they have to scroll and hunt for proof that you're legitimate.
  • The page loads very slowly
    On a typical mobile connection, your website takes 8–9 seconds to show content. Most people leave after 3 seconds. Google's data shows that 53% of mobile visitors abandon a site if it takes longer than 3 seconds to load. Slow speed is costing you enquiries every single day.
  • Google and AI search engines can't understand your site
    Modern search engines (including ChatGPT and Google's AI) rely on structured information to recommend businesses. Your website is missing this layer entirely, which means you're invisible when people ask AI "Who's a trusted roofer in Brisbane?" You're losing a growing share of searches.
