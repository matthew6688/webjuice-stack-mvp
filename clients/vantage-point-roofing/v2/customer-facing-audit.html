What's Working Well

  • You've earned serious trust. 138 Google reviews at 4.6 stars is exactly what local customers look for. That social proof is worth its weight in gold.
  • Your branding looks professional. The logo is clear, the hero message mentions your 10-year workmanship guarantee, and desktop visitors can see your phone number straight away.
  • You've built real search presence. With 161 pages covering different services and Brisbane suburbs, you've done the legwork to show up when people search for specific roofing work in specific areas.

What's Holding You Back

  • Your mobile visitors can't easily ring youUrgent
    On a phone, your header only shows a small phone icon—no visible number. Most Brisbane roofing searches happen on mobile, and if people can't see your number in the first three seconds, they'll tap back to Google and call the next company.
  • No clear "next step" button when people land on your site
    Your homepage has good information, but there's no obvious "Get a Quote" or "Call Now" button in the first screen—especially on mobile. People who are ready to act right now don't know where to click, so they leave.
  • Your enquiry form asks for too much
    The quote form has 10 fields. Industry testing shows that anything over 4 fields cuts your conversion rate in half. People start filling it out, get tired, and abandon it halfway through.
  • Your site loads slowly on phones
    You're carrying 10 tracking scripts that add up to nearly half a megabyte. On a real 4G connection, that means a 5–8 second wait before people see anything useful. Every extra second of load time loses roughly 10% of your visitors.
You haven't had website data for two years. Your site still has Google's old Universal Analytics installed, which stopped collecting information in July 2023. That means you've been flying blind—no idea which pages work, where people drop off, or whether your Google Ads are actually bringing in calls.

What Changes When We Fix This

  • More phone calls from Google. A visible tap-to-call button on mobile means people who are standing in front of a leaking roof can reach you in one tap, not three.
  • More quote requests. A shorter form (name, suburb, phone, job type) gets filled out 2–3× more often than a long one. You'll hear from people who would have given up halfway before.
  • Faster site, fewer people bouncing. Clean up the tracking scripts, optimise your images for mobile, and the site loads in under 2 seconds. That keeps people around long enough to see why you're worth calling.
  • You'll know what's actually working. Proper analytics setup means you can see which services get the most interest, which suburbs send you traffic, and whether your marketing spend is paying off.
  • Better Google rankings over time. Google's started using page speed and mobile experience as ranking factors. A faster, cleaner site doesn't just convert better—it shows up higher in search results.

The bottom line: You've done the hard work of building a trusted roofing business with real customers and real reviews. Your website just needs to get out of the way and let people contact you.
