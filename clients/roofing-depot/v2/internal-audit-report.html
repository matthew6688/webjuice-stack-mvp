CRITICAL desktop_403_fatal
Desktop site shows 403 Forbidden — site unreachable

观察到The desktop screenshot renders a light-blue error page with a padlock icon on a monitor graphic, bold teal text reading '403 - Forbidden', and a subtitle 'Access to this page is forbidden.' No business content is visible at all.

为什么是问题Any customer who finds this business on Google Maps or runs a desktop search and clicks the website link sees an error page, not the business. They will assume the business has closed down or is disreputable and immediately search for a competitor. Desktop accounts for roughly 40–50% of local commercial searches.

正确长啥样A live, loading homepage with the business name, phone number, and at least one category of products or services visible within 2 seconds on any modern browser on desktop.

redesign 怎么改Resolve the server 403 at origin (check Cloudflare or hosting IP allow-lists, .htaccess deny rules, or CDN geo-blocking). Confirm desktop access before launch. Add an uptime monitor (e.g. UptimeRobot) so outages are caught in minutes.

CRITICAL no_phone_above_fold_mobile
No phone number visible anywhere on mobile above fold

观察到The mobile header contains four icons only: hamburger menu, search magnifier, person/account icon, and a shopping cart icon. There is no phone number, no 'Call Us' button, and no click-to-call link visible in the header or the hero section.

为什么是问题A customer who has just searched 'roofing Gold Coast' and landed on this site on their phone wants to call immediately to ask about stock, pricing, or whether the store is open. If there is no phone number visible, many will bounce back to Google and call a competitor who shows their number prominently. Studies show 60–70% of local mobile searchers call directly from the search results or a website within minutes of landing.

正确长啥样A sticky mobile header with the phone number formatted as a tappable tel: link (e.g. '07 XXXX XXXX' in white text) pinned to the top bar so it is always visible when scrolling, alongside the logo.

redesign 怎么改Add a tappable phone number as a persistent element in the mobile header bar (right-aligned or below the logo). Style it as a high-contrast button (e.g. orange or yellow on black) so it reads as an action item, not a label.

CRITICAL no_location_or_suburb_visible
No Gold Coast suburb or address shown on mobile

观察到The mobile hero section shows a photo of a warehouse building with 'ROOFING DEPOT' text and a single CTA button. No street address, suburb name, or map reference appears anywhere in the visible portion of the page.

为什么是问题A trade customer or walk-in buyer searching for a Gold Coast roofing supply store needs to verify the business is local before committing to a visit. Without seeing 'Burleigh, Molendinar, Nerang' or a suburb name, they cannot confirm it is actually on the Gold Coast. They will go back to Google to check the Maps listing instead of converting.

正确长啥样Suburb name and full address displayed in the header or directly below the hero (e.g. 'Serving the Gold Coast — 12 Example St, Molendinar QLD 4214'), with a tap-to-map link.

redesign 怎么改Add a thin location bar below the main header ('📍 Molendinar, Gold Coast — Open Mon–Fri 7am–5pm') or embed the suburb prominently in the hero headline. Link it to Google Maps.

MAJOR zero_trust_signals_mobile
No reviews, credentials, or social proof visible on mobile

观察到The entire visible mobile page — header, hero, and three product category cards — contains no star ratings, Google review count, years in business, supplier accreditations, or any other social-proof element.

为什么是问题A first-time customer from a Google search has no reason yet to trust this business over a competitor. Without a review count or badge ('★ 4.8 — 120 Google Reviews'), they cannot quickly verify the business is reputable. Trade buyers especially will hesitate before placing an order with an unknown supplier.

正确长啥样A trust bar immediately below the hero (or within it) showing Google star rating + review count, years trading, and any brand partnerships (e.g. Colorbond, BlueScope logos). On mobile this can be a horizontally scrolling strip of 3 trust pills.

redesign 怎么改Add a three-item trust bar below the hero: [★ 4.x Google Rating | XX Years in Business | Authorised [Brand] Supplier]. Pull the star rating dynamically from Google or hardcode the current verified figure.

MAJOR weak_hero_cta
Hero CTA 'Check Out Our Products' is passive browse language

观察到The sole call-to-action button in the mobile hero reads 'Check Out Our Products' in white text inside a dark rounded rectangle, overlaid on the warehouse photo.

为什么是问题The phrase 'Check Out' frames the visit as casual browsing, not purposeful action. A tradesperson who needs materials urgently or a homeowner looking for a specific panel type does not want to browse — they want to find their item fast or speak to someone. This language creates no urgency and does not address what the customer actually needs.

正确长啥样Two CTAs in the hero: a primary high-contrast button ('Shop by Category' or 'Find Your Materials') and a secondary link ('Call to Check Stock — 07 XXXX XXXX'). The primary button should be a solid accent color (orange, yellow) so it reads as an action, not a label.

redesign 怎么改Replace 'Check Out Our Products' with 'Shop All Categories' (orange fill) and add a secondary ghost button 'Call to Check Stock' that links to the phone number. Position both buttons stacked vertically so they are thumb-reachable on mobile.

证据截图 · Desktop 800px region

MAJOR all_nav_hidden_hamburger
All navigation buried behind hamburger menu — contact inaccessible

观察到The mobile header has a hamburger (three horizontal lines) icon as the only navigation trigger. No top-level links to 'Contact', 'Location', or 'About' are visible without tapping the menu.

为什么是问题A customer who needs to find the store address, trading hours, or a phone number must perform an extra tap, then scan a hidden menu, before finding any contact path. Each friction step loses a percentage of visitors, especially older trade customers who may not be confident with hamburger menus.

正确长啥样A sticky header with the phone number visible at all times and, at minimum, a visible 'Contact / Find Us' link or button. The hamburger can remain for secondary navigation but contact must never be hidden.

redesign 怎么改Keep the hamburger for the full menu but pin a phone button (tap-to-call) and a location link permanently in the header bar. On mobile, right-align a phone icon that opens a tel: link immediately.

证据 · 移动端截图

MINOR cramped_three_column_mobile_grid
Three-column product cards on mobile cause cramped, small text

观察到Below the hero, three category cards (Tools, Consumables, Architectural Panels) are displayed in a three-column grid on a ~375px wide mobile screen. Category titles are bold but the sub-item lists ('Seaming, Measuring, Snips, Hammers, & Scribers') render at approximately 10–11px in the leftmost column, which is visibly smaller than comfortable reading size.

为什么是问题Small text on mobile forces users to pinch-zoom or squint to read the product sub-categories. Tradespeople scanning quickly for a specific product type (e.g. silicone accessories) may miss it entirely and assume the business doesn't stock it.

正确长啥样A two-column card layout on mobile with 14px+ body text, a clear product image, category name in 18px bold, and a concise 2–3 item sub-list. Three columns work on tablet (768px+) but not on phone screens.

redesign 怎么改Change the product category grid to two columns on screens below 600px. Increase sub-item font size to minimum 14px. Consider removing the sub-item list entirely on mobile and replacing with a single strong sub-headline (e.g. 'Hand tools for every roofing job').

需要保留的优点

  • Mobile header is clean and dark with good contrast — the logo is legible and the overall color palette (black/white) is professional and consistent with a trade-supply brand.
  • The hero image of the actual physical warehouse builds authenticity — real-place photography is more trustworthy than stock photos and should be preserved.
  • Product category cards use real product photography which grounds the offering and helps trade customers quickly identify what the business stocks.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Fix the desktop 403 immediately — every day it is broken, all desktop-sourced leads are going to competitors.
  2. 2. Add a visible, tappable phone number and suburb/address to the mobile header so local search visitors can verify and contact the business without hunting.
  3. 3. Insert a trust bar (Google star rating, years trading, brand accreditations) directly below the hero to give first-time visitors a reason to stay and buy.
