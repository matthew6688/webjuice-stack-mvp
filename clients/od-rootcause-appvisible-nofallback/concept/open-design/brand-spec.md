## Observed posture

- Dark, ember-led palette with warm stone and marble cues rather than bright restaurant whites.
- Logo is white on dark; typography can carry ceremony through serif headlines and compact uppercase metadata.
- Existing navigation intent: Menus, Main Menu, Lunch Specials, banquets, drinks, functions, gift vouchers, gallery, contact, careers, and bookings.
- Primary conversion is SevenRooms bookings; secondary paths are menu exploration, functions enquiry, bar/drinks, and contact.
- Brand voice is theatrical and provenance-led: fire, lamb, Mediterranean dining, live seafood tanks, dry-aging lamb cabinet, 140-bottle wine list, 120-seat room, private 10-seater.
