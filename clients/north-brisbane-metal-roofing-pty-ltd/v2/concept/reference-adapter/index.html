FAQ

Common questions about metal roofing in Brisbane.

If your question isn't here, call us — we'll tell you straight whether it's worth a site visit.

Do you do residential and commercial metal roofing?

Yes. We install new metal roofs on residential new builds, re-roof existing homes, and handle commercial sheds, warehouses and industrial buildings. Domestic and commercial work both require QBCC licensing — we hold both.

What does a metal re-roof cost in Brisbane?

A standard residential re-roof in Colorbond typically ranges from $12,000–$25,000 depending on roof area, pitch, material choice and access. We provide a fixed written price after the site inspection — never an estimate over the phone.

Can you repair a leaking metal roof, or does it need full replacement?

Most metal roof leaks are repairable — failed sealant around penetrations, lifted sheets, or corroded flashings. We inspect, locate the leak, and recommend repair or replacement based on the extent of damage and remaining roof life.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — QBCC licensed for residential and commercial roofing, full public liability insurance, and workers compensation. We provide copies of all documentation with every quote.

How long does a metal roof installation take?

A typical residential new roof or re-roof takes 2–5 days depending on size and complexity. Larger commercial projects are scheduled based on square metreage. We provide a clear timeline in the written quote.
