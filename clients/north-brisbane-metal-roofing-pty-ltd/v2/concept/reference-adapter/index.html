FAQ

Straight answers to the questions builders and homeowners ask.

If yours isn't here, call us — we'll give you a clear answer on the phone.

Colorbond or Zincalume — which should I choose?

Colorbond comes pre-painted in 20+ colours with a factory finish that lasts 15+ years. Zincalume is bare metal with better coastal corrosion resistance but no colour. Most Brisbane residential jobs use Colorbond; coastal and industrial projects often spec Zincalume.

What does a typical residential metal roof replacement cost?

A standard 3-bedroom Queenslander or brick home is typically $12,000–$18,500 depending on pitch, profile, insulation and gutter replacement. We give a fixed written price after the site measure — never an estimate over the phone.

Can you work to builder schedules and progress claims?

Yes — we coordinate with your site program, provide progress invoicing, and submit compliance documentation (QBCC, insurance, material certificates) at practical completion.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — QBCC licensed, $20M public liability, and full workers compensation. We provide copies of all certificates with every quote.

Do you handle council approvals and building certifications?

For replacement roofs on existing homes, most councils accept a complying development approval. For new builds, your builder's certifier handles roof sign-off. We provide all compliance documentation required.
