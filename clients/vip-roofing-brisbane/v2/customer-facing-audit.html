What's Working Well

You've built real trust with your customers

Your Google Maps listing shows 25 five-star reviews. That's genuine proof that you do excellent work, and it's exactly what potential customers are looking for when choosing a roofer.

Your business name is perfectly set up for local search

The domain name "viproofingbrisbane.com.au" includes both your service (roofing) and location (Brisbane). That's valuable — it tells search engines and customers exactly what you do and where you operate.

People are already finding you on Google

Your Google Maps presence is active and sending traffic your way. The foundation for getting enquiries is there — you just need somewhere proper for those visitors to land.

What's Holding You Back

Right now, your website has one critical problem that's costing you every single visitor who clicks through from Google: it's not actually a business website.

Your website is a "for sale" parking page

What this means: When someone clicks on your website link from Google Maps or search results, they don't see your business. Instead, they see a black page that says "viproofingbrisbane.com.au may be for sale" with a bunch of links to other roofing companies.

What this costs you: Every single visitor thinks your business has closed down or the link is broken. 100% of them leave immediately and call a competitor instead.

The page actively sends visitors to your competitors

What this means: Those big buttons on the parking page — "TOP METAL ROOFING COMPANIES NEAR ME", "ROOF FIXING SERVICES" — aren't links to your services. They're paid advertisements that send people to search results full of your competitors.

What this costs you: You're paying for the domain, but it's working as a free advertising board for every other roofing company in Brisbane. Every click is a lost job.

There's no way for anyone to contact you

What this means: The parking page has no phone number, no email address, no contact form, no physical address — nothing. Even if someone wanted to give you the benefit of the doubt, they couldn't reach you.

What this costs you: Research shows that over 70% of people searching for local tradespeople want to call straight away. With no phone number visible, you lose every one of those ready-to-book customers.

No proof of your work or credentials

What this means: The page shows no photos of completed jobs, no customer testimonials, no licence information, no mention of how long you've been in business — nothing to prove you're a real, trustworthy company.

What this costs you: 93% of customers check reviews and credentials before choosing a tradie. Without any trust signals, even visitors who somehow stick around won't take the next step.

Mobile visitors see completely unrelated content

What this means: On a mobile phone, the parking page shows ads for "walk-in showers for bathrooms" and "book your own appointment online" — nothing to do with roofing at all.

What this costs you: Mobile users give a website about 3 seconds to prove it's relevant. Seeing bathroom renovation ads, they assume they've clicked the wrong link and leave instantly.

What Changes When We Fix This

A proper website doesn't have to be complicated — but it does need to be real. Here's what happens when visitors land on an actual business site instead of a parking page:

Every Google click becomes a potential phone call. When your phone number is front and centre on both desktop and mobile, formatted so people can tap to call, you turn browsers into callers. Even a simple one-page site with a big "Call Now" button will dramatically increase your enquiries.

Your 25 five-star reviews start working for you. Right now, those reviews sit on Google Maps, but visitors who click through to your website never see them again. Displaying your rating and a few customer quotes on your site reminds people why they should choose you.

You stop losing customers to competitors. Instead of sending people away to other roofing companies, your site keeps them focused on your services, your credentials, and your contact details. Every visitor who would have clicked a competitor ad instead calls you.

You look like the professional business you are. A real website with photos of your completed work, your areas of expertise (metal roofing, tile repairs, gutter work, etc.), and your licence details builds instant trust. Customers can see you're legitimate, experienced, and local.

Mobile visitors — your biggest opportunity — can actually use your site. Most people search for tradies on their phone, often while standing in front of a leaking roof or after a storm. A mobile-friendly site with a tap-to-call button captures these high-intent customers the moment they need you.
