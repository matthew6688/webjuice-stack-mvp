FAQ Sample · editable

Common questions about roofing services in Brisbane.

If your question is not here, call us — we will give you a straight answer.

Restoration or full replacement — how do I know which I need?

Roof age matters less than condition. A 40-year tile roof with sound structure is usually a restoration job. Concrete tiles that have softened, or metal roofing with through-rust, normally need replacement. The inspection report tells you which camp you are in, with photos.

What does roof restoration typically cost in Brisbane?

A standard 3-bedroom home is typically $4,500–$8,000 depending on tile condition and access. We give a fixed written price after the inspection — never an estimate over the phone.

I think I have a leak — can you come this week?

Yes. We prioritise emergency repairs and storm call-outs. Phone is the fastest way: (07) 3062 7779. We respond same-day for urgent cases.

Can you work directly with my insurance company?

For storm damage and accidental events, yes — we provide the photo report, itemised quote, and scope-of-works your insurer needs.

How long does a typical roof restoration take?

Most residential roof restorations take 2–4 days depending on roof size and weather. We provide a timeline estimate with your quote.
