## Layout posture

- Use a black/charcoal dominant canvas with white wordmark, uppercase navigation, and square button geometry.
- Photography should do the luxury work: steak, seafood, private dining, marble, wine, and low-lit interiors.
- Keep the accent restrained: whisky/bronze for the booking CTA, menu highlights, and thin dividers.
- Avoid soft SaaS cards; use editorial panels, fine borders, large image crops, and generous vertical spacing.
- Preserve existing site intent: direct paths to menu, functions, vouchers, parking/contact, and external booking.
