设计观感的整体诊断

MAJOR dated_hero_overlay
Hero design feels dated

观察到The desktop hero uses a pale washed-out roof photo with a large angled translucent white overlay cutting through the middle of the image.

为什么是问题A local customer comparing roofers may read this as an older website that has not been refreshed in years, which can make the business feel less active or less premium.

正确长啥样A sharp real roofing project photo with a simple dark or light overlay, clear headline, visible service area, and one strong phone or quote button above the fold.

redesign 怎么改Replace the faded angled hero treatment with a high-resolution Brisbane roof project image, flat overlay for text contrast, and a single primary CTA block.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR weak_trust_above_fold
Trust proof is missing above fold

观察到The first desktop screen shows the logo, phone number, menu, hero headline, and buttons, but no review rating, licence detail, years in business, or completed-job proof.

为什么是问题Roofing is a high-cost, high-trust service. A Google-searching visitor wants quick reassurance that the business is legitimate before calling.

正确长啥样Above the fold should include 3-4 compact trust markers such as star rating, insured/licensed wording, Brisbane service area, and number of roofs completed.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust strip directly under the header or inside the hero with Google rating, licensed and insured claim, local Brisbane coverage, and a proof metric.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MINOR desktop_cta_competes
CTA labels compete

观察到The desktop header has a wide coral bar saying “BOOK YOUR FREE ROOF CONSULTATION” while the hero button below says “BOOK A FREE CONSULTATION”.

为什么是问题Two similar calls to action in different places make the page feel repetitive and less deliberate, instead of guiding the visitor to one obvious next step.

正确长啥样One primary action should dominate, with the phone number as the immediate alternative for urgent roof problems.

redesign 怎么改Use one consistent primary CTA label across header and hero, such as “Get a Free Roof Quote”, and pair it with a click-to-call phone button.

证据截图 · Desktop 800px region

CRITICAL mobile_no_phone_number
Mobile hides the phone number

观察到On mobile, the top bar shows only a phone icon and hamburger icon; the actual number “1300 734 148” is not visible.

为什么是问题A phone-searching customer with a leaking or damaged roof often wants to call immediately. Hiding the number behind an icon adds doubt and friction.

正确长啥样Mobile header should show a tappable phone button with either the full number or a clear “Call 1300 734 148” label visible without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Replace the standalone phone icon with a visible click-to-call control reading “Call 1300 734 148” or add a sticky bottom call bar.

MAJOR mobile_hero_text_crowded
Mobile hero text is cramped

观察到The mobile hero stacks “BRISBANE ROOF REPLACEMENTS” and “AND BUILDING PROFESSIONALS” over a busy roof image, with divider lines and a large angled overlay taking much of the space.

为什么是问题On a small screen, the message takes effort to read and does not quickly answer what the company does, where it works, and why to trust it.

正确长啥样Mobile should use a single-column hero with a short headline, one supporting line, readable 16px+ text, and a primary call button visible immediately.

redesign 怎么改Simplify the mobile hero to “Brisbane Roof Replacement & Repairs” with one support line, stronger contrast, and remove decorative divider lines.

MAJOR mobile_service_list_overwhelms
Service list dominates mobile

观察到Immediately under the mobile “QUICK QUOTE” bar, six dark service rows fill the screen with icons, uppercase labels, and chevrons.

为什么是问题The list is useful, but it pushes trust proof, reviews, photos, and contact reassurance out of view. A visitor may see options before feeling confident to act.

正确长啥样Mobile should show the main CTA, then a compact trust row, then 3-4 top services with a “View all services” option.

redesign 怎么改Keep only the highest-intent services near the top, add review/licence proof above the list, and move the full service menu lower on the page.

MINOR desktop_social_sidebar_noise
Social sidebar feels old

观察到The desktop screenshot shows a small vertical social media bar floating on the right edge with Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube icons.

为什么是问题For an urgent local roofing decision, these icons distract from calling or requesting a quote and make the interface feel like an older template.

正确长啥样Social links should be placed quietly in the footer, while the right side or sticky area is reserved for phone and quote actions.

redesign 怎么改Remove the floating social sidebar and use that attention for a sticky call/quote control or leave the hero visually cleaner.

需要保留的优点

  • The business name and phone number are prominent on desktop.
  • The coral quote button stands out clearly against the muted page colors.
  • The mobile service rows make the main roofing services easy to scan.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Make mobile contact immediate with a visible click-to-call number and sticky quote/call action.
  2. 2. Rebuild the hero with sharper roofing imagery, clearer Brisbane service wording, and stronger text contrast.
  3. 3. Add trust proof above the fold: reviews, licence/insurance wording, local proof, and project credibility.
