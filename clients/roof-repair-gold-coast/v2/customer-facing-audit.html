What's Holding You Back

We've identified several issues that are directly costing you customers. Here are the most important ones:

Your website shows the wrong business
This is the most serious issue: your site currently displays content for "Alumniapp" – a university alumni app – instead of your roofing business. The logo, headline, images, and navigation all reference an app product. When customers click through from Google Maps expecting to see a Gold Coast roofing company, they see campus buildings and smartphone mockups instead. Most visitors will assume they've reached the wrong site and click back within seconds.
"Not Secure" warning in the browser
Your site doesn't have HTTPS security enabled. Every visitor sees a "Not Secure" label in their browser's address bar, and some browsers show a full-screen warning before they can even view your site. Beyond damaging trust, this directly hurts your Google ranking – sites without HTTPS have been penalised in search results since 2018.
Phone number isn't easy to find
When someone lands on your site from Google, they can't see your phone number without scrolling. On mobile, there's no click-to-call button at all. Research shows that 60–70% of local service customers prefer to ring rather than fill in a form. If they can't find your number immediately, many will simply go back and call the next roofer in the search results.
No licence or insurance information visible
Queensland law requires roofers to hold a QBCC licence, and customers know this. Your site doesn't display your QBCC licence number, ABN, insurance details, or any other trust credentials. When customers are comparing roofers, they'll choose the one that looks more transparent and legitimate – especially for a job that costs thousands of dollars.
No Google reviews showing
Your Google Business Profile shows 0 reviews. While you have a 4.7-star rating, customers trust businesses with a visible track record. Even a handful of genuine reviews can make the difference between a phone call and a pass.
Slow loading on mobile
Google's own speed test gave your mobile site a performance score of 47/100. The page takes over 10 seconds to fully load on a typical 4G connection. Most people won't wait that long – they'll hit the back button and try another roofer.
