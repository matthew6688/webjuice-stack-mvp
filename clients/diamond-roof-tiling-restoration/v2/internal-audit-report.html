CRITICAL mobile_cta_not_clear
Phone action is not clear on mobile

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the phone number appears as a large blue glossy graphic reading “1300 4 DIAMOND” under the QBCC text, with no plain “Call now” button visible.

为什么是问题A Google-searching roofing customer on their phone wants the fastest path to call. A decorative number graphic looks like an image or banner, so some visitors may not realize they can act immediately.

正确长啥样A fixed or above-the-fold mobile call button with plain text such as “Call 1300 4 DIAMOND”, high contrast, 44px+ tap height, and a secondary “Request quote” button.

redesign 怎么改Replace the glossy phone-number image with a real tappable call button in the mobile header and add a second quote button directly below the hero/service message.

MAJOR outdated_visual_style
Glossy graphics feel old

观察到The desktop header uses a black background, blue outlined logo, glossy blue phone-number badge, bevelled active Home tab, and multiple shiny award medallions across the top.

为什么是问题The design looks like an older website, which can make visitors wonder whether the business is still actively maintained, even if the company itself is reputable.
