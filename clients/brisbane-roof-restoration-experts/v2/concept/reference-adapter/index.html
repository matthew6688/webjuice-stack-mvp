Honest answers to the questions homeowners actually ask.

If yours is not here, call us — we'll tell you straight whether it is worth a visit.

Do you handle small repair jobs?

Yes. Many roofers only want full restoration jobs. We handle minor repairs — a few lifted tiles, small leak points, gutter fixes. If it is a small job, we will quote it fairly and fit it in quickly.

How fast can you quote?

Most quotes within 24 hours of inspection. We inspect, take photos, and send a clear written quote — no vague estimates over the phone.

What does a roof restoration cost in Brisbane?

A standard 3-bedroom home is typically in the range of several thousand dollars depending on tile condition and access. We give a fixed written price after inspection — never an estimate over the phone.

Are you insured?

Yes — full public liability and workers compensation. We will send copies before you sign anything.

Can you work with my insurance company for storm damage?

Yes — we provide the photo report, itemised quote, and scope of works your insurer needs.
