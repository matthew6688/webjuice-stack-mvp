## Layout posture

- Dark charcoal background with white logo and white navigation; avoid pale luxury unless used as a text panel contrast.
- Use real restaurant photography as the first-viewport signal; keep overlays minimal and readable.
- Montserrat all-caps/small-caps works for navigation, labels, and menu categories; larger display type should stay measured and spaced.
- Borders and dividers should be thin, warm, and low-contrast; avoid heavy rounded cards.
- Accent budget: one warm gold action and one fine editorial rule per screen.
- Preserve sitemap intent with clear paths to menu, bookings, contact/location, functions/private dining, gallery, gift vouchers.
