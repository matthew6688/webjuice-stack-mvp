视觉审计 · Vision LLM

设计观感的整体诊断

The website uses a dated layout with a cluttered header and a hero section that relies on a generic stock photo, making the business feel less professional than it likely is.

MAJOR cluttered_header_navigation
Header is too busy and distracts from the phone number

观察到The top navigation bar contains 8 distinct menu items (Home, About Us, Our Services, etc.) plus social icons, a search icon, and contact details, all crammed into a single horizontal strip.

为什么是问题A visitor looking for a roofing quote is overwhelmed by choices. The phone number is buried in the top strip rather than being a primary call-to-action. The sheer number of links suggests a lack of focus on the main goal: getting a quote.

正确长啥样A simplified header with a logo on the left, a 'Get a Quote' button on the right, and a hamburger menu for secondary links. The phone number should be prominent and clickable.

redesign 怎么改Reduce navigation items to 3-4 key categories. Move the phone number to a sticky header or make it a large button. Remove social icons from the main nav to reduce visual noise.

证据截图 · Element: header, nav

MAJOR generic_stock_hero_image
Hero image uses a generic stock photo instead of real work

观察到The main banner image features a smiling family pointing upwards in a generic room, overlaid with a blue tint and text. It does not show any actual roof renovations or the company's work.

为什么是问题Customers searching for 'roofing Brisbane' want to see proof of capability. A stock photo feels impersonal and untrustworthy. It doesn't demonstrate the specific service (attic renovation) effectively.

正确长啥样A high-quality photo of a real, completed attic renovation project by the company, showing the 'before and after' or the finished space. Alternatively, a photo of the team at work.

redesign 怎么改Replace the stock photo with a real project image. Use a split-screen 'before/after' slider or a high-res photo of a finished attic space with the headline overlaid clearly.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

CRITICAL weak_call_to_action
Primary call-to-action button is buried and low contrast

观察到The 'Get in touch for a free quote!' button is yellow, placed at the bottom of the hero section, and requires scrolling to see fully on mobile. It is not the most prominent element.

为什么是问题The main goal is to get a quote. The button is not immediately visible or compelling. On mobile, users might not scroll down to find it. The yellow color blends with the text and doesn't stand out enough against the blue background.

正确长啥样A large, high-contrast button (e.g., bright orange or white with dark text) placed above the fold, directly under the main headline. It should say 'Get Your Free Quote' or 'Call Now'.

redesign 怎么改Move the CTA button to be immediately visible under the headline. Increase its size and contrast. Add a secondary CTA like 'Call 07 3889 6688' for immediate contact.

证据截图 · Desktop 800px region

MINOR outdated_typography_and_colors
Font choices and color scheme feel dated

观察到The logo uses a mix of sans-serif and script fonts. The color palette includes a dark teal header and yellow accents, which feels a bit generic and not particularly modern.

为什么是问题While not broken, the design language feels like it hasn't been updated in 5-7 years. This can subconsciously signal to customers that the business is not current or innovative.

正确长啥样A cleaner, more modern font pairing (e.g., a strong sans-serif for headings, a readable sans-serif for body). A refined color palette that uses white space more effectively.

redesign 怎么改Update the logo slightly for a cleaner look. Use a modern sans-serif font throughout. Increase white space to make the content breathe.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

需要保留的优点

  • The tagline 'Same house, more home' is catchy and clearly communicates the value proposition.
  • The contact information (phone number and address) is present in the header, which is good for local SEO.
  • The headline 'Bigger. Brighter. Better.' is clear and benefit-driven.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Replace the generic stock photo with a real project image to build immediate trust.
  2. 2. Simplify the header and make the phone number/quote button the most prominent element.
  3. 3. Move the primary CTA button above the fold and increase its visual weight.
