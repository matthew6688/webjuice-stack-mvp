What's Working Well

Your business name is clear. On mobile, visitors immediately see "Sydney Roofing Specialists" when they land on your site.

Your phone number is on the page. It's visible in an orange bar near the top on mobile devices, which is a good start for getting calls.

You have a perfect Google rating. Five stars is excellent — though right now it's not being used to its full potential (more on that below).

What's Holding You Back

Your desktop site shows an error page instead of your business
When someone searches for roofing services on their laptop or work computer and clicks through to your site, they see a "403 - Forbidden" error message on a blank blue screen. That's it. No business name, no phone number, nothing. These visitors close the tab within seconds and call your competitor instead. You're losing roughly half your potential enquiries before they even see what you do.
Browsers warn customers your site is "not secure"
Your website doesn't have HTTPS security, so Chrome, Safari, and Firefox all show a "Not Secure" warning in the address bar. Many visitors — especially older homeowners who are cautious online — will close the page immediately. Even those who stay are less likely to trust you with their phone number or email. This one issue alone is probably costing you 30–40% of your traffic.
Visitors can't figure out how to contact you quickly
There's no obvious "Call Now" or "Get a Quote" button in the first few seconds. Your phone number is there, but it's tucked into the layout and not immediately clickable. When someone's roof is leaking and they're searching on their phone in a panic, they need a big, clear button that dials your number with one tap. Right now, they're more likely to hit the back button and try the next business.
The mobile homepage tries to say too much at once
Your mobile homepage crams a long paragraph, a large heading, a big Google rating graphic, and a call button into one small space. It feels cluttered. Visitors scan it for two seconds, can't find what they need, and leave. A simpler layout with one clear message — "We fix and replace roofs across Sydney" — and one big call button would get you far more enquiries.
Your five-star rating has zero reviews listed
You're showing a five-star Google rating, but there's no number of reviews next to it. Visitors assume it's fake or that you've only had one job. Even if your rating is real, it doesn't build trust without a review count. If you genuinely have reviews, we need to show them. If you don't yet, we need to start collecting them — because reviews are the number one factor in whether someone calls you or keeps scrolling.

What Changes When We Fix This

More phone calls from serious customers. When your site loads properly on every device, shows a clear call button, and feels secure, you'll see a noticeable jump in enquiries — often within the first week.

Higher trust from day one. A secure site with real project photos, visible licensing details, and genuine customer reviews makes people feel confident they're calling a legitimate, professional business.

Better visibility on Google. Fixing the technical issues (security, mobile layout, page speed) helps Google rank you higher in local search results. More people see you, more people click, more people call.

You stop losing customers to competitors. Right now, your website is sending visitors to other roofing companies. A working, modern site keeps them on your page and turns them into paying customers.
