What's Holding You Back

Here are the specific issues costing you enquiries right now:

Your site shows "Not Secure" warnings
Because your website doesn't use HTTPS, browsers display a "Not Secure" warning in the address bar. Some browsers even block the page entirely. Most visitors close the tab immediately when they see this warning. This also hurts your Google ranking—sites without HTTPS have been penalised since 2018.
Your phone number is hard to find
On both desktop and mobile, visitors have to scroll or hunt through menus to find how to call you. For roofing emergencies, most customers want to phone straight away. If they can't find your number in 3 seconds, they'll go back to Google and call the next business.
You have zero Google reviews showing
Google ranks businesses partly on review volume—not just the star rating, but how many reviews you have. With zero reviews visible, you're starting behind competitors who actively collect them. A simple post-job SMS asking customers to leave a review would fix this.
The text is hard to read on mobile
White text over a pale roof photo blends in, especially on phones in sunlight. If visitors can't quickly read what you do, where you work, or how to contact you, they leave. First impressions matter—and mobile is where most local searches happen.
Google can't understand your business properly
Your site is missing the structured data that tells Google (and AI tools like ChatGPT) who you are, where you're located, and what services you offer. That means you're invisible in Google's "near me" results and knowledge panels—the high-conversion spots where customers click to call.
