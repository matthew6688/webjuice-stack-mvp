CRITICAL washed_out_hero
Hero image severely overexposed, face and text barely visible

观察到The main hero photo showing a plumber working under a sink has extreme white overlay/washout effect making the person's face, the work being done, and background details almost completely bleached out and illegible

为什么是问题A local customer searching 'joe's plumbing Melbourne' lands here and cannot see the actual tradesperson or work quality; the washed-out effect makes the business look unprofessional or like a stock photo site rather than a real local company with real technicians

正确长啥样Hero image at normal exposure showing clear, recognizable plumber performing work with visible branding on uniform; customer can see the person's face clearly and the quality of work being demonstrated; image feels documentary-style and authentic

redesign 怎么改Replace with properly exposed photo of actual Joe's Plumbing technician at work site; remove all white overlays; ensure face and uniform logo are clearly visible; use subtle dark gradient only if needed for text contrast

MAJOR cluttered_top_bar
Social media icons crowd header, no clear visual hierarchy

观察到Desktop header has dark navy top strip containing 'We specialize in Plumbing' text on left and five small social media icons (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube) aligned right

为什么是问题A customer coming from Google Business Profile wants to call or book immediately; social media links in prime header real estate distract from conversion actions and make the business look like it's trying too hard to appear modern rather than focusing on service

正确长啥样Header reserves top-right space for phone number only (large, tappable, with click-to-call); social proof comes from reviews or certifications, not social icons; clean single-row header with logo left, phone right

redesign 怎么改Remove social icons from header entirely; move phone number from secondary position to primary top-right with contrasting background color; social links go to footer only

MAJOR desktop_nav_contrast
Blue navigation bar blends with header, poor visual separation

观察到Desktop navigation bar uses medium blue (#3498DB approximate) directly below the darker navy header bar, with white text for 'HOME' and 'GET APPOINTMENT' at opposite ends

为什么是问题The two different blue bars create visual confusion about where the actual navigation starts; a visitor scanning quickly cannot distinguish the clickable menu from decorative header elements, increasing cognitive load when they just want to find services or contact info

正确长啥样Single-row header with white or light gray background; navigation links in dark text color; primary CTA button (Get Appointment) in distinct accent color with adequate spacing; clear visual break between branding and navigation

redesign 怎么改Consolidate to one header row: white background, logo left, text navigation in center (Services, About, Contact), phone + CTA button right; use single accent color for CTA only

MAJOR logo_quality
Logo appears pixelated and amateur with inconsistent elements

观察到The logo shows an American flag on the left, 'J P' letters in a white circle center, 'Joe's Plumbing, Inc.' text curved around the circle, 'Chicago' below, and five red stars on the right; the overall composition looks compressed and the stars/flag feel disconnected from the plumbing brand

为什么是问题A Melbourne customer searching for local plumbing sees 'Chicago' in the logo and American flag imagery which creates immediate geographic confusion and suggests the site may be a franchising template not customized for the local market; this breaks trust before they even read the content

正确长啥样Clean, vector-quality logo with 'Joe's Plumbing' wordmark and simple plumbing icon (wrench, pipe, water drop); location reference either removed or correctly shows 'Melbourne'; professional typography; logo works at small sizes without pixelation

redesign 怎么改Commission new logo designed specifically for Melbourne market; remove American flag and Chicago reference; use simple icon + wordmark format; ensure logo is provided as SVG for sharp rendering at all sizes

MAJOR mobile_sticky_header_bulk
Mobile sticky header consumes 35% of screen with redundant elements

观察到Mobile version has dark teal sticky header taking up approximately 180-200px showing 'CALL US NOW 1 (773) 688 9242' at top, search bar with 'Enter Search Keywords' placeholder in middle, then logo and hamburger menu below

为什么是问题On mobile screens where every pixel matters, this header blocks more than one-third of the viewport before any actual content appears; the search bar is unnecessary for a local service business with 4-5 pages, and the stacked layout pushes the hero and conversion content far down

正确长啥样Compact mobile header maximum 60-80px height with logo left, phone-icon right (tap to call), hamburger menu; no search bar; minimal padding; allows hero and primary CTA to appear in first screen

redesign 怎么改Redesign mobile header as single thin row: small logo + phone icon + menu icon; remove search entirely; phone number appears when icon is tapped; reduce height to 60px; ensure hero CTA visible above fold

MINOR cta_button_generic
CTA text 'Get Appointment' is passive and unclear

观察到Desktop navigation shows 'GET APPOINTMENT' in white text on blue background at far right; hero section has a red/coral button labeled 'REQUEST AN APPOINTMENT'

为什么是问题Generic appointment language doesn't create urgency or specify what happens next; a customer with a leaking pipe wants immediate help, not a scheduled appointment; the passive phrasing reduces click-through compared to action-oriented alternatives

正确长啥样Primary CTA says 'Call Now for Emergency Repair' or 'Get Same-Day Service' with phone number visible immediately below; secondary CTA can be 'Book Online' for non-urgent work; language matches customer's mental state (urgency, problem-solving)

redesign 怎么改Replace 'Get Appointment' with 'Call Now: [phone]' as primary CTA; add secondary 'Book Online' button below; update hero button to 'Get Help Now' or 'Emergency Service Available'; use first-person or urgent language

MINOR mobile_hero_carousel
Mobile hero has carousel arrows but unclear if functional

观察到Mobile screenshot shows hero image with left and right chevron arrows (< >) overlaid on the photo, indicating a carousel/slider interface

为什么是问题Carousels on mobile are notoriously problematic—customers rarely swipe through them, and having multiple hero messages dilutes the primary conversion goal; the arrows add visual clutter and accidentally tapped arrows frustrate users trying to scroll

正确长啥样Single static hero image on mobile with one clear message and one primary CTA; no carousel; full-width image optimized for mobile aspect ratio; message and button remain visible without requiring interaction
