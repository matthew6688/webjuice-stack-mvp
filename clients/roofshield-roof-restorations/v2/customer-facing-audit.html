What's Holding You Back

Your reputation is strong, but your website isn't doing it justice. Here are the specific issues costing you customers every week:

1. Your website shows "Not Secure" in the browser

Your site doesn't have HTTPS, so when someone clicks through from Google, their browser warns them the site is "Not Secure." Over 80% of people close the tab immediately when they see that warning. You're losing calls before they even read a word.

2. Your phone number is hidden

Most people searching for roof restoration want to call, not fill out a form. But your phone number isn't visible when the page first loads — visitors have to scroll to find it. If they can't see how to contact you in 2 seconds, they'll click back to Google and call someone else.

3. The navigation is overwhelming

There are 9+ menu items crammed across the top of your site, making it hard to find anything quickly. When people feel confused, they leave. Research shows cluttered navigation can reduce enquiries by up to 40%.

4. The main photo looks generic

Your homepage shows a stock photo of a house — not your actual crew, not a real Brisbane project, no branding. Local customers want proof you're a real local business with real local work. Generic images make people doubt you're legitimate.

5. The headline is hard to read

Your main text is red on a blue sky background, making it difficult to read — especially on a phone in sunlight. If visitors can't quickly understand what you do, they won't stay long enough to find out.
