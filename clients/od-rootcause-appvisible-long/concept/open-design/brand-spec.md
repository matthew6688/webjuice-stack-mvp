## Layout Posture

- Dark, theatrical restaurant identity: near-black backgrounds, white logo, low accent budget.
- Headings are uppercase on the live site; concept keeps uppercase metadata and some sectional titles, while using serif display type for luxury.
- Thin rules and marble-like surfaces suit the existing menu styling better than heavy cards.
- Photography should lead: embers, lamb, seafood, dining room, stone, and fire.
- Copy tone is mythic and sensory: fire, flock, embers, provenance, lamb, seafood, and ritual.
