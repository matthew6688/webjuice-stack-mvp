What's Holding You Back

Here's where things get interesting. Your website has some issues that are directly costing you jobs — but the good news is, they're all fixable.

Your site shows a security warning Critical

The problem: Your website doesn't have HTTPS security. When someone visits your site, their browser displays a "Not Secure" warning in the address bar. Some browsers even show a full-screen warning before letting people through.

What this costs you: Google has been lowering search rankings for non-secure sites since 2018. More importantly, 8 out of 10 visitors will leave immediately when they see that warning — especially for a service like roofing where trust is everything. With zero Google reviews to back you up, that security warning is killing your credibility before anyone even reads your content.

Mobile visitors can't find your phone number

The problem: When someone opens your site on their phone, they see your logo, a menu icon, and a headline — but no visible phone number or call button on the first screen. They have to scroll down to find it.

What this costs you: Most people searching for roofing services are on their phone. If they can't find how to call you within 8 seconds, they hit the back button and ring your competitor instead. Every hidden phone number is a lost job.

The builder watermark hurts your credibility

The problem: There's a "Made with Manus" badge sitting in the bottom corner of your site. It looks like the website isn't finished yet.

What this costs you: Roofing is a high-trust service. Homeowners are inviting you onto their property and trusting you with one of the most expensive parts of their house. A builder watermark makes your business look less professional, and that costs you enquiries.

You're invisible to Google's local search

The problem: Your website is missing the technical code (called LocalBusiness markup) that tells Google you're a local business. It's also missing proper page titles and the kind of structure that Google looks for.

What this costs you: When someone searches "roof repairs Gold Coast", Google doesn't know where you're located, what your phone number is, or when you're open. You're missing out on the map listings, knowledge panels, and AI-generated answers that your competitors are appearing in. This is free visibility that you're leaving on the table.

You have zero Google reviews

The problem: Your Google Business Profile shows 0 reviews. Your competitors have dozens.

What this costs you: Review count is one of Google's ranking signals for local search — not just the star rating, but how many you have. Beyond rankings, most homeowners won't call a roofer with zero reviews when there are others with 20+ reviews right next to you. A simple SMS campaign after every completed job could fix this in 90 days.
