FAQ

Honest answers to the questions homeowners actually ask.

If yours isn't here, call us — we'll tell you straight whether your roof needs work or not.

Does my roof need restoration or just cleaning?

A good pressure wash can restore a roof that looks tired but has solid pointing and no cracked tiles. If the mortar is crumbling or tiles are lifting, restoration (re-bedding and re-pointing) is needed. The inspection will show you which camp you're in.

What does roof restoration actually cost in Brisbane?

A standard 3-bedroom Brisbane home is typically $3,500–$6,500 depending on roof size, tile condition, and access. We give a fixed written price after the inspection — never an estimate over the phone.

How long does a roof coating last?

A quality two-coat acrylic or membrane system typically lasts 10-15 years in Brisbane conditions before needing a refresh. Cheaper single-coat products may only last 3-5 years.

Do you work on Colorbond roofs as well as tile?

Yes — we pressure wash, repair rust spots, and apply protective coatings to Colorbond and metal roofs. Tile (terracotta and concrete) is our most common job, but we handle all residential roof types.

Can I get an inspection this week?

Usually yes — we keep slots free for inspections and urgent repairs. Phone is the fastest way: 0410 607 076. We'll arrange a time that suits you.
