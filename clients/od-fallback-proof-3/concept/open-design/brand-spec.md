## Observed posture

- Dark, theatrical hospitality brand: black-brown ground, ivory logo/text, fire-warm accent.
- Editorial restaurant copy: mythic, sensory, provenance-led language around lamb, embers, seafood, wine, and gathering.
- Layout should feel refined and cinematic: large serif headings, thin rules, generous negative space, and immersive food/venue photography.
- Navigation intent to preserve: menus, banquets, drinks, functions, gift vouchers, gallery, contact, careers, and SevenRooms bookings.
- Core facts to preserve: Mediterranean restaurant at The Star Brisbane; The Terrace, Level 4; all day dining, 7 days, 11am-late; phone (07) 2111 6869; free validated parking; 10% weekend surcharge and 20% public holiday surcharge.
