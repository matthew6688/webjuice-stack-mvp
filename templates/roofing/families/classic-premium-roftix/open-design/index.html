FAQ & contact

Questions homeowners ask before a roof visit.

The answers are written to be useful without promising facts the company has not verified.

Do I need a repair or a replacement?

That depends on the age, material condition, water path, and number of failing details. A good visit should separate urgent fixes from larger replacement planning.

Can you look at one problem area?

Yes. The page supports small, high-intent repair requests such as flashing, valleys, penetrations, and isolated storm wear.

What should I prepare before a visit?

Photos of the issue, interior leak locations, attic access notes, and any previous repair information help the roofer understand the concern faster.
