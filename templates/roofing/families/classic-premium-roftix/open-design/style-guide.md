# Roftrix Roofing Style Guide

## Design Tokens

- `--bg`: `#F7F5F0` warm editorial background.
- `--surface`: `#FFFFFF` content cards and form panels.
- `--fg`: `#212121` primary reading text.
- `--muted`: `#51606F` captions, footer text, body support copy.
- `--border`: `#E3DFD6` warm hairlines and card borders.
- `--accent`: `#0E6B4F` primary action green.
- `--primary`: `#0B2F57` cinematic roof navy.
- `--warm`: `#FF6F3C` small secondary accent for the logo mark only.
- `--ink`: `#101417` deep footer charcoal.

## Typography

- Display: `"Playfair Display", "Iowan Old Style", "Charter", Georgia, serif`.
- Body: `"Inter", -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", system-ui, sans-serif`.
- Mono: `ui-monospace, "JetBrains Mono", "SF Mono", Menlo, monospace`.

Use the serif display face for all major headings. Keep body copy practical and calm.

## Logo

Use one default demo direction only: a simple roofline/service mark with readable `RR` wordmark. Do not create alternate customer-choice logo options.

## Layout Rules

- Lead with roof photography in the first viewport.
- Keep CTAs green and direct: "Book a Roof Visit", "Request a Roof Visit".
- Use project imagery and service detail photos before icons.
- Do not add fake reviews, fake years, fake warranties, fake licenses, fake prices, or fake addresses.
- Footer should include service paths, page paths, and placeholders for verified service areas.
