Quick answers

Can I ask about one problem area?

Yes. Start with the area you can see: flashing, valleys, vents, shingles, storm wear, or an interior leak spot.

What details help before a visit?

Photos, leak locations, roof age if known, and any previous repair notes help the team understand the concern faster.
