Questions

Fast answers before the roof visit.

Do you handle active roof leaks?

Yes. The contact path is set up for leak triage, temporary protection, and follow-up repair planning without promising an unverified emergency window.

Can this template support residential work too?

Yes. The layout leads with commercial authority but the service rows can include residential replacement, repairs, gutters, and storm damage support.

What should we prepare before a walkthrough?

Photos of the leak area, access notes, known roof age if available, and any maintenance history help the contractor start with context.

Can we start without a full roof history?

Yes. Photos, access notes, and the current concern are enough to begin. Any unknowns can be sorted during the walkthrough.
