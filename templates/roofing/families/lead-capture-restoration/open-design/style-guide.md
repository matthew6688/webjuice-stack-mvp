# RidgeLine Roof Restoration Style Guide

## Tokens

- `--bg`: `#FAF3E8` warm neutral page background.
- `--surface`: `#FFFDF8` form and card canvas.
- `--fg`: `#1D2328` charcoal contrast band, text, footer.
- `--muted`: `#6B6258` warm grey support copy.
- `--border`: `#E8DCCA` warm divider line.
- `--accent`: `#C9472A` roof-red action color.

## Typography

- Display: Inter-style sturdy sans, heavy weights for direct local-service clarity.
- Body: Inter/system sans, 16px base, generous line height.
- Labels: uppercase mono or strong small sans for service chips and proof labels.

## Logo

Use one default demo direction only: a simple geometric roof/service mark plus readable RidgeLine Roof Restoration wordmark. Do not create alternate logo options, badges, award seals, or copied reference marks.

## Layout Rules

- Phone and inspection CTA must be visible above the fold.
- Hero uses the wide roofer-on-metal-roof image as the primary visual.
- Service/process proof uses the second roofer image.
- Metal panel images are supporting material visuals only.
- Do not turn the page into a product catalogue.
- Do not invent reviews, licenses, awards, years in business, street address, or email.

## Components

- Primary CTA: roof-red background with white text, 44px minimum height.
- Hero surface: charcoal panel with warm-white text and restrained 16px radius.
- Service modules: simple service blocks with practical customer-facing copy.
- Contact form: name, phone, service need, roof details.
