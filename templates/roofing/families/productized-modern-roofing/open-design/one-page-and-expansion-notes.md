# Roofex Roofing Systems Template Notes

## Clean One-Page Version

Use `index.html` as the primary one-page template. It is structured for a roofing company that wants visitors to compare material options, understand service categories, and request a quote from one page.

Recommended one-page section order:

1. Hero with wide modern metal roof image and clear quote path
2. Service-category trust strip
3. Material/product cards for metal, terracotta, and composite roofing
4. Service accordion with roofer-on-metal-roof image
5. Drone inspection proof panel and four-step process
6. Project/gallery teaser
7. FAQ accordion
8. Quote CTA and request form
9. Calm footer with oversized wordmark

The one-page version should stay focused on customer decisions. Avoid adding badges, claims, reviews, awards, licenses, prices, or addresses unless a real business provides verified details.

## Simple Multi-Page Expansion

The same design language can expand into these pages:

- `home`: Condensed hero, materials preview, service preview, quote CTA.
- `about`: Company story, crew image, values, service area, verified credentials if provided.
- `services`: Full service accordion expanded into cards and comparison modules.
- `service-detail`: One service per page, with scope, signs to look for, inspection notes, and quote CTA.
- `projects`: Gallery index using roof type, material, and project category filters.
- `project-detail`: Photo-led case story with problem, material choice, scope, and final result.
- `faq`: Expanded question library grouped by inspections, materials, replacement, repair, and scheduling.
- `blog`: Roofing advice index for seasonal care, material education, and inspection preparation.
- `blog-detail`: Article page with clear headings and quote CTA.
- `appointment`: Focused quote request page with contact details, roof notes, and project type.

## Content Guidance

- Keep copy customer-facing and practical.
- Use material comparisons as conversion tools.
- Use drone and roof-service photography where proof or process needs credibility.
- Do not invent prices, warranty terms, review counts, licenses, addresses, or years in business.
- Replace demo contact copy with verified business information before publishing.
