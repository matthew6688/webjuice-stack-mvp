Questions

Before you book

Do you inspect roofs across both Brisbane and the Gold Coast?

Yes. RidgeLine Roof Co. presents this service area as Brisbane and the Gold Coast. Confirm suburb availability when you call or send the enquiry.

Can you quote without seeing the roof?

Photos and notes help triage the issue, but roof access, material condition, and safety requirements usually need a site inspection before quoting accurately.

What should I include in the enquiry?

Include the suburb, roof type if known, the visible issue, when it started, and whether there are active leaks or storm-related concerns.

Do you handle gutters as well as roofing?

Yes. Gutter repairs are part of the listed services, especially where overflow or drainage is affecting the roofline.
