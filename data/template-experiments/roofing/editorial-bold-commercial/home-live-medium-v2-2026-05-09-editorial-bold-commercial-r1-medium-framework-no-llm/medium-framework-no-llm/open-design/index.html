Questions before you book

Roofing FAQs for Brisbane and Gold Coast properties.

Can I request an inspection before choosing a repair?

Yes. The enquiry can start with a roof inspection, especially when the source of a leak or drainage issue is not obvious from ground level.

Do you handle both homes and commercial roofs?

Yes. The service mix covers residential and commercial enquiries, including repairs, restoration planning, metal roofing and gutter issues.

What should I include in the form?

Include the suburb, roof type if known, what changed, access notes, and whether the enquiry is urgent after weather damage.
