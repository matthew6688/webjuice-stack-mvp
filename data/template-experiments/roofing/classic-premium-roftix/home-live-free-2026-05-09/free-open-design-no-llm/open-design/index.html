Roofing questions homeowners ask first.

Can a leak be repaired without restoring the whole roof?

Sometimes. The inspection should identify whether the issue is localised, tied to flashing or guttering, or part of broader surface wear.

What should I include in my enquiry?

Your suburb, roof material if known, where the issue appears, and whether water is entering the home. Photos can help when available.

Do you cover both Brisbane and the Gold Coast?

Yes. RidgeLine Roof Co. handles roofing enquiries across Brisbane and the Gold Coast.
