FAQ

Common roofing questions.

Can I request a roof inspection before deciding on repairs?

Yes. The enquiry should begin with what you are seeing, where the issue appears, and whether it is urgent. A roof inspection or quote path can then be recommended.

Do you handle both repairs and restoration?

Yes. The listed services include roof repairs, roof restoration, metal roofing, gutter repairs, and roof inspections for Brisbane and Gold Coast homeowners.

What should I include in the enquiry?

Share the suburb, roof concern, property type, preferred contact method, and any safe notes about access or visible damage.

Can project photos and customer reviews be added?

Yes. Real project captions, customer reviews, licence details, and insurance information can be added once the business has supplied the confirmed details.
