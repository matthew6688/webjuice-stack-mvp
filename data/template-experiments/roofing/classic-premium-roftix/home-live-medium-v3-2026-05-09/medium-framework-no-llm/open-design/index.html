Questions homeowners ask

Useful answers before you send the form.

Can I ask about a small leak?

Yes. Small roof issues can still need prompt attention, especially around flashing, valleys, vents, and gutters.

Do I need to know the roof material?

No. If you know it is tile or metal, include that. If not, describe the problem and the team can scope the next step.
