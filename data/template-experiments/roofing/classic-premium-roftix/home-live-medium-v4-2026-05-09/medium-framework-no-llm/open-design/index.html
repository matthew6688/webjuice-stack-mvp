Concrete work cues

A roof scope should show what was checked, not just what was quoted.

The strongest roofing decisions come from visible conditions: flashings, ridges, gutters, roof sheets, drainage paths, and surface preparation.

  • Before and after structureUse project photos to compare roof condition, access, preparation, and finished presentation.
  • Material-specific notesSeparate repair, restoration, and metal roofing recommendations instead of bundling everything into one vague quote.
  • Clear exclusionsIf something needs further access or specialist confirmation, name it clearly before approval.
