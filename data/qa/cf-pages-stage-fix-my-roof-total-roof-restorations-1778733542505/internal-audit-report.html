CRITICAL cookie_banner_blocks_content
Cookie consent banner covers primary business info

观察到A large black cookie consent modal in the bottom-left corner covers approximately 20% of the viewport, obscuring the business description text that starts with 'DINO from FIX MY ROOF...'

为什么是问题A visitor from Google search who lands here to learn about the roofing service cannot read the critical service description without first dismissing a cookie banner. Many mobile users will bounce rather than hunt for a close button. This creates immediate friction at the moment of highest intent.

正确长啥样Cookie notices should be a thin banner at top or bottom (max 60px height) with a single 'Accept' action, or deferred until scroll/interaction. Core business information (services offered, service area, credentials) must be immediately readable without requiring any dismissal action.

redesign 怎么改Remove cookie banner entirely for static marketing site (no tracking cookies = no banner needed), or use minimal top-bar notice that doesn't obscure content.

Relying on GBP interface instead of owned website

观察到This is a Google Business Profile screenshot showing Google's standard template layout with purple header, business hours widget, and default social icons. There is no evidence of a custom website design or branded landing page.

为什么是问题Google Business Profile is a directory listing, not a conversion-optimized sales page. Mobile-first design with phone CTA above fold.

MINOR business_hours_widget_takes_prime_real_estate
Hours widget more prominent than service description

观察到The 'Open 6:30 AM - 7:00 PM' widget with arrow is positioned above the business name and description, occupying the most prominent slot in the upper content area.

为什么是问题Visitors searching 'roofing Brisbane' want to know what services you offer and why to choose you, not your hours (which they assume are standard weekday business hours). Prioritizing hours over services/trust signals wastes attention in the critical first 3 seconds.

正确长啥样Hours should be small text in footer or header corner (e.g. 'Mon-Sat 6:30am-7pm'). Top priority content: headline promise ('Brisbane Roof Restorations — 15 Years, 500+ Happy Homes'), service list, and phone CTA.

redesign 怎么改Move hours to header top-right as small text. Use prime above-fold space for value proposition headline, 3-column service icons (Pressure Washing, Ridge-Capping, Coating), and 'Call Now for Free Quote' button.

MINOR social_icons_without_proof
Generic social media icons without engagement context

观察到Five circular social media icons (phone, message, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok) are displayed in a row below the business name, all in black circles with white symbols, no labels or follower counts.

为什么是问题Icons without context do not build trust. A visitor cannot tell if these accounts have 50 followers or 5,000, or if they are active. Clicking to 'check' is friction most won't bother with. If the accounts have strong engagement (e.g. '1,200 followers, daily Brisbane project posts'), that is a trust signal being hidden.

正确长啥样If social accounts have meaningful followings (500+ with regular local content), display as 'Follow us: Instagram (1.2K followers) | Facebook (800 reviews)'. If accounts are sparse, remove icons and prioritize phone/quote form instead.

redesign 怎么改Audit social accounts. If strong: add follower counts and testimonial snippets from comments. If weak: remove icons, replace with 'As seen on Google (4.8★ from 120 reviews)' trust badge.
