CRITICAL mobile_cta_not_visible
No phone or quote button on mobile

观察到In the mobile screenshot, the top of the page shows only the large Ace Roofing Service logo, then the hero text; there is no visible phone button or quote button above the fold.

为什么是问题A Google-searching Brisbane customer is often trying to call quickly, especially for roof leaks or urgent repairs. If they cannot see a tap-to-call action immediately, they may go back to the search results and call another roofer.

正确长啥样Mobile header with a compact logo and a sticky tap-to-call button visible at the top, plus a second quote button directly under the main headline.

redesign 怎么改Reduce the mobile logo height, add a sticky bottom or top phone CTA reading 'Call 0426 366 7xx', and place a 'Get Free Quote' button directly below the hero headline.

证据 · 移动端截图

CRITICAL desktop_phone_number_truncated
Phone number appears cut off

观察到In the desktop header, the blue phone button shows '0426 366 7...' with the final digits hidden or cropped.

为什么是问题A visitor may worry the site is unfinished or may not be able to copy the number accurately. For a local trade business, an unclear phone number immediately weakens trust and makes calling harder.

正确长啥样A full phone number displayed in one line with enough button width, strong contrast, and no ellipsis.

redesign 怎么改Widen the desktop phone button and set the phone text to never truncate; use a clear label such as 'Call 0426 366 7xx' with a phone icon.

MAJOR headline_too_large_on_mobile
Mobile headline overwhelms screen

观察到On mobile, the headline takes most of the visible screen height and wraps into many short lines before any contact action appears.

为什么是问题A phone visitor has to work through a long sales line before seeing what to do next. This slows down decision-making when the customer mainly wants to know whether the roofer is local, trustworthy, and easy to contact.

正确长啥样A shorter mobile headline in 32-40px type, followed immediately by one sentence of service detail and a visible call or quote button.

redesign 怎么改Rewrite the mobile hero to 'Brisbane Roof Repairs & Restoration' with a smaller support line about licensed, insured service and 10-year protection, then show phone and quote CTAs.

MAJOR hero_image_too_dark
Roof image is overly dark

观察到The hero background is a very dark roof photo with a heavy navy overlay, making the actual roofing work hard to inspect.

为什么是问题Customers hiring a roofer want visual proof that the business does real, quality work. A dark image feels more like a generic template than evidence of local roofing results.

正确长啥样A brighter real project photo showing a clean Brisbane roof, with the text placed on a controlled dark panel or gradient only where needed for readability.

redesign 怎么改Replace the current dark hero image with a sharp completed-roof project photo and use a lighter overlay limited to the text area.

MINOR desktop_header_shape_distracts
Angled logo block feels dated

观察到The desktop header places the logo inside a large white angled block that cuts diagonally into the hero image.

为什么是问题The unusual angled shape draws attention away from the phone and quote actions. It can also make the design feel older than the actual business.

正确长啥样A simple horizontal header with compact logo on the left and phone/quote buttons aligned on the right, using consistent spacing.

redesign 怎么改Replace the angled white logo panel with a clean sticky header bar, keeping the logo smaller and the contact actions visually dominant.

证据截图 · Full desktop

MAJOR trust_claims_not_prioritized
Trust points are scattered

观察到The desktop hero lists many checkmark claims across two columns, including licensed, insured, family-owned, emergency needs, and 20 years expertise.

为什么是问题The useful proof is present, but it is buried in a long list. A local customer scanning quickly may miss the strongest reasons to trust the business.

正确长啥样Three compact trust badges near the CTA: 'Licensed & Insured', '20+ Years Experience', and 'Emergency Roofing 24/7', followed by a short service line.

redesign 怎么改Condense the checklist into three high-priority badges directly under the CTA, then move secondary claims lower on the page.

需要保留的优点

  • The business name and roofing category are clear in both desktop and mobile screenshots.
  • The blue accent color gives the phone and '10 Years' promise strong visibility.
  • The page already includes useful trust claims such as licensed, insured, family-owned, and emergency availability.

Redesign 优先级

  1. 1. Add visible mobile tap-to-call and quote CTAs above the fold.
  2. 2. Fix the desktop header so the full phone number is readable and contact actions dominate.
  3. 3. Replace the dark generic hero treatment with a brighter real roofing project image and three focused trust badges.
