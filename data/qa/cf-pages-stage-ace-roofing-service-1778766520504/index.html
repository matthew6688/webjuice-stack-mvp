FAQ

Common questions about our roofing services.

If you have other questions, please call us — we're happy to provide honest advice about your roofing needs.

Do I need restoration or full replacement?

It depends on your roof's condition. A roof with good structural integrity but cosmetic wear is usually a candidate for restoration. If there's significant deterioration, rust-through, or structural issues, replacement may be necessary. Our inspection will tell you which option makes sense for your situation.

What does a typical roofing job cost?

Every roof is different, so we provide quotes after inspection rather than estimates over the phone. Costs depend on roof size, condition, access, and the type of work required. Our quotes are detailed and fixed — no surprises.

How quickly can you come out for a leak?

We prioritise emergency calls and leaks. In most cases we can visit within 1-2 business days, often sooner for urgent situations. Call us directly on 0449 168 985 for the fastest response.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are a fully licensed roofing contractor and carry appropriate insurance coverage. We're happy to provide documentation before starting any work.

Can you help with insurance claims?

Yes, for storm damage and insured events we can provide the documentation and assessments your insurer requires to process your claim.
