Where You're Losing Customers Right Now

Here's the challenge: mobile visitors make up more than half of local searches, and most people call within minutes of finding you. Your website isn't set up to capture those quick decisions.

No call button on mobile

When someone finds you on their phone, they can't tap to call. They have to hunt for your number, and most won't bother – they'll just ring the next company on the list.

What this costs you: Fewer calls, fewer quotes, jobs going to competitors who make it easier to get in touch.

Chat box blocks important content

The live chat widget covers your hero image and call-to-action on mobile. It looks cluttered and makes the site harder to use.

What this costs you: Visitors leave before they understand what you do or how to contact you.

No trust signals above the fold

Your first impression shows marketing copy and a slogan, but no review rating, no licence badge, no before-and-after proof. Roof and attic work is expensive – people need to see evidence before they'll trust you.

What this costs you: Hesitant visitors who choose companies that look more established, even though you have better reviews.

Missing the basics for search visibility

Your site is missing the technical foundations that help you show up in local searches and on Google Maps. Things like local business data, properly formatted headings, and search-friendly page titles.

What this costs you: Lower rankings, fewer people finding you when they search for roof renovations in Brisbane.

Contact form asks for too much

Your quote form has eight fields including first name, last name, suburb, and a dropdown menu. Most people give up before they finish.

What this costs you: Lost enquiries from people who would've contacted you with a simpler form.

What Changes When We Fix This

A redesigned website built around your strengths and your customers' behaviour would:

  • Make it easy to call you – prominent tap-to-call button on every screen, especially mobile
  • Put your reputation front and centre – 5-star rating, review quotes, and real project photos visible immediately
  • Simplify the contact process – a short quote form (name, phone, brief message) that people actually complete
  • Show up in more searches – proper technical setup so Google and other search tools can find and recommend you
  • Load faster on mobile – compressed images and clean code so visitors don't wait around
  • Keep your existing search rankings – every page redirected properly so you don't lose the ground you've already gained

The goal isn't to change what you do – it's to make sure your website reflects the quality and professionalism your customers already experience.
