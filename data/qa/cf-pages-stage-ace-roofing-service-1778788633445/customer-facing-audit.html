What's Working Well

Your website already has some solid foundations in place:

  • Your site loads quickly. Google gave your mobile site a performance score of 95 out of 100, which puts you ahead of many competitors. Fast sites keep visitors from bouncing back to search results.
  • You're building trust with the right credentials. Visitors can see you're licensed, insured, family-owned, and offer emergency service. These are exactly the reassurances people look for when choosing a roofing company.
  • Your message is clear. People landing on your site immediately understand you're a Brisbane roofing company with over 20 years of experience.

What's Holding You Back

Here are the specific issues that are costing you customers right now:

  • Your phone number is hidden on mobile
    Most roofing searches happen on a mobile phone, often when someone has a leak or storm damage. When they land on your site, there's no tap-to-call button at the top—they have to scroll down and manually type your number. Many will simply hit back and call the next company instead. This is probably your biggest missed opportunity.
  • Your desktop phone number looks cut off
    On a computer, your header shows "0426 366 7..." with the rest hidden. It looks unfinished and makes it harder for people to call you. First impressions matter, especially when someone is comparing you to other roofers.
  • You have no Google reviews
    Google ranks businesses with more reviews higher in local search results. Even five reviews can move you up the page. More importantly, when people compare you to competitors with dozens of reviews, they're more likely to choose the other company—even if your work is better.
  • Google can't fully understand your business
    Your website is missing some technical markers that tell Google you're a local roofing business. Without these, you won't appear in Google's map cards or knowledge panels—those are the high-visibility spots that get the most clicks. You're also invisible to newer AI search tools like ChatGPT when people ask for roofing recommendations.
  • Your hero image is too dark to see the work
    The main background photo on your homepage has a heavy dark overlay, so visitors can't actually see the quality of your roofing work. People want to see real Brisbane roofs you've completed—clear, bright photos build trust faster than generic dark backgrounds.

What Changes When We Fix This

Here's what you can expect when these issues are addressed:

  • More phone calls from mobile visitors. Adding a prominent tap-to-call button at the top of every page makes it effortless for people to ring you the moment they're ready.
  • Better first impressions. A complete, visible phone number and clearer trust badges help visitors feel confident choosing you over competitors.
  • Higher rankings in local search. With proper technical setup and a plan to collect reviews, Google will show your business to more people searching for roofing services in Brisbane.
  • Visibility in new search channels. When someone asks ChatGPT or Google's AI "who's a good roofer in Brisbane?", your business will actually be in the running instead of invisible.
  • More quote requests. Clear calls-to-action, visible contact buttons, and real project photos working together means more of your visitors turn into paying customers.
