FAQ Sample · editable

Straight answers to the questions Brisbane homeowners ask.

If yours isn't here, call 1800 173 885 — we'll tell you straight whether it's worth a visit.

Restoration or full replacement — how do I know?

Age matters less than structure. A 40-year tile roof with sound battens and no major sag is usually a restoration job. Softened concrete tiles, through-rust on Colorbond, or sagging frame lines normally mean replacement. The inspection report shows which camp you're in, with photos.

What does a roof restoration cost in Brisbane?

A typical 3-bedroom Queenslander or brick-and-tile is $4,800–$8,500 depending on tile condition, access, and scope. We give a fixed written price after the inspection — never an over-the-phone estimate.

I think I have a leak — can you come this week?

Yes. We prioritize emergency repairs and storm call-outs. Phone 1800 173 885 is the fastest way. After-hours we'll schedule you for the next morning.

Do you provide photo evidence for insurance claims?

Yes — for storm damage and accidental events we provide a photo pack, itemised quote, and scope-of-works your insurer needs. We can liaise directly with your assessor if needed.

What about warranty issues after the work is done?

All restoration and replacement work carries a written workmanship warranty. If you notice an issue within the warranty period, call us and we'll schedule a site visit to assess and rectify at no charge.
