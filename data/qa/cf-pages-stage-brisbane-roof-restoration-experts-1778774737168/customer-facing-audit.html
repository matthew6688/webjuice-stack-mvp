What's Holding You Back

The quote form is overwhelming

Right now, your form asks visitors to tick boxes for 13+ different services before they can even submit their details. Research shows that half of people will give up and leave when a form looks this complicated — they just want to leave their phone number and have you call them back.

What this costs you: If 100 people visit your site each week and 50 of them would have enquired with a simple form, you're potentially losing 25 enquiries every week just because the form feels like too much work.

Visitors can't see your Google reviews on the site

You've earned a 4.9-star rating, but when someone lands on your website, they can't see it. No star rating, no "21 five-star reviews", no mention of how long you've been in business. Without these trust signals visible immediately, about 35–45% of visitors will click back to Google to find a competitor whose site shows their credentials upfront.

What this costs you: People searching for roof restoration are cautious — it's an expensive job. If they can't quickly verify you're legitimate and well-reviewed, they move on.

The design looks a bit dated

The heavy black shadows around your headline text are a style from about ten years ago. While this doesn't affect how well your business performs the work, it does affect how professional visitors think you are in the first eight seconds they're on the page. Younger homeowners and property managers especially notice when a site looks like it hasn't been updated in a while.

What this costs you: Studies show 30–40% of people will leave a site within seconds if the design feels outdated, even if the business itself is excellent.

No proof that you're a real local Brisbane business

The background image is a generic house photo — it could be anyone, anywhere. There's no photo of your truck, your team, or a real Brisbane project you've completed. About 25–35% of visitors specifically look for this kind of proof before they trust a tradie website, especially for high-value work like roof restoration.

What this costs you: Without visible evidence that you're actually operating in Brisbane (a branded vehicle, your crew on site, a recognisable local home), people worry they might be dealing with a lead-generation company or an interstate contractor, not a genuine local team.

Your form has no spam protection

There's currently nothing stopping automated bots from flooding your enquiry form with junk submissions. When you're getting five fake enquiries for every real one, it's easy to miss the genuine customers or become skeptical of all form leads.
