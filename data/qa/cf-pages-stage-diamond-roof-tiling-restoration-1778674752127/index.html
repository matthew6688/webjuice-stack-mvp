FAQ Sample · editable after sign-up

Honest answers to questions Brisbane homeowners ask.

If your question isn't here, call us — we'll give you straight advice about what your roof needs.

Do I need a full restoration or just repairs?

Every roof is different. That's why we start with a thorough inspection. If repairs will do the job, we'll tell you that. We won't recommend a full restoration unless it's genuinely needed — and we'll show you the photos to explain why.

What does roof restoration cost in Brisbane?

It depends on the size, condition and access. A typical 3-bedroom Brisbane home restoration ranges from reasonable to mid-range pricing. We provide a fixed written quote after inspection — never vague estimates over the phone.

How long does restoration take?

Most Brisbane home restorations take 3-5 days depending on size and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline with your quote and keep you updated throughout the job.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed, insured and compliant. We provide all documentation with your quote and operate to Queensland building standards.

Will you be on-site during the work?

Yes. Sam personally works on every restoration and supervises the entire job from start to finish. That's a guarantee you'll get from us that you won't get from larger companies.
