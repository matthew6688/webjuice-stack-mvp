设计观感的整体诊断

The site has a clear local roofing message and visible phone CTA, but the dated hero treatment, weak trust proof, and cramped quote form reduce confidence.

MAJOR no_visible_trust_proof_above_fold
No trust proof above the fold

观察到The desktop hero shows the logo, navigation, headline, call button, and quote form, but no review rating, license badge, warranty message, years in business, or completed-project proof is visible.

为什么是问题A Google-searching local customer comparing roofers needs quick reassurance that this is a real, reputable Brisbane business before calling or sharing contact details.

正确长啥样Above the fold includes a compact trust row such as Google rating, licensed and insured, Brisbane-based, warranty length, and a small completed roof photo or review snippet.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust strip directly under the hero headline or above the form with Google star rating, review count, warranty badge, service area, and one short customer quote.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR dated_shadowed_hero_text
Hero text looks dated

观察到The large white hero headline uses heavy grey drop shadows over a muted roof photo background.

为什么是问题The shadowed text feels older and less refined, which can make a visitor wonder whether the business is current and detail-oriented enough for a high-value roof job.

正确长啥样A sharper hero with clean white or charcoal type, controlled contrast overlay, 48-64px desktop headline, and no heavy text shadow.

redesign 怎么改Replace the heavy text-shadow treatment with a darker image overlay or solid content panel area, then set the headline in a clean bold font with tighter spacing.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

MAJOR desktop_form_feels_cramped
Quote form is visually crowded

观察到The desktop quote form sits on the right in a semi-transparent dark block with many checkboxes, radio buttons, yellow labels, and a large text box all visible at once.

为什么是问题A visitor looking for a quick quote may feel the form is work before they have decided to trust the business, so they may choose the easier phone call or leave.

正确长啥样A shorter quote form with name, phone, suburb, and one service dropdown first, plus optional details after submission or in a second step.

redesign 怎么改Redesign the hero form as a compact four-field form with one primary red 'Get free quote' button, moving roof age, scope, and work type into a follow-up step.

证据截图 · Element: form

CRITICAL mobile_contact_options_hidden
Mobile quote form is hidden

观察到The mobile screenshot shows only the logo, menu icons, headline, supporting copy, and red call button; the quote form is not visible without scrolling.

为什么是问题Many local roofing visitors arrive from their phone and may prefer requesting a quote instead of calling immediately; if the only visible action is a phone call, quieter or after-hours visitors may not act.

正确长啥样Mobile first screen shows a sticky call button and a second 'Get quote' button, with a short form or quote entry point visible immediately below the hero.

redesign 怎么改Add two mobile CTAs under the headline: 'Call now' and 'Get free quote', then place a compact quote form directly after the hero with a sticky bottom call bar.

Hero image lacks proof
