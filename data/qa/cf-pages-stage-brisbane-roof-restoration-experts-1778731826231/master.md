**正确长啥样**

Mobile first screen shows a sticky call button and a second 'Get quote' button, with a short form or quote entry point visible immediately below the hero.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Add two mobile CTAs under the headline: 'Call now' and 'Get free quote', then place a compact quote form directly after the hero with a sticky bottom call bar.


### 主要问题 · 4 项（影响转化的明显短板）

### 主要 · homepage_title_clear

**技术事实**

title='# Brisbane Roof Restoration Specialists' contains-name=true contains-niche=false

**普通话翻译**

你网站的浏览器标签 title 没把业务名字 + 服务关键词写清楚（比如该写「Brisbane Roof Restoration Experts - roofer Brisbane」，但目前是泛泛一句）。

**对客户的影响**

Google 搜索结果里展示的就是这个 title。写不清楚 = 排名靠后 + 即使排上来客户也不知道是不是匹配的服务。SEO 最便宜的修复，但很多本地企业完全没做。


### 主要 · No trust proof above the fold

**技术事实**

The desktop hero shows the logo, navigation, headline, call button, and quote form, but no review rating, license badge, warranty message, years in business, or completed-project proof is visible.

**普通话翻译**

首页第一屏没有马上告诉客户“这家公司靠不靠谱”，比如评分、保修、资质或真实评价都没出现。

**对客户的影响**

本地客户通常会同时打开2到3家屋顶公司比较；如果8秒内看不到可信证据，他们更可能回到Google地图点下一家，直接减少来电机会。

**正确长啥样**

Above the fold includes a compact trust row such as Google rating, licensed and insured, Brisbane-based, warranty length, and a small completed roof photo or review snippet.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Add a trust strip directly under the hero headline or above the form with Google star rating, review count, warranty badge, service area, and one short customer quote.


### 主要 · Hero text looks dated

**技术事实**

The large white hero headline uses heavy grey drop shadows over a muted roof photo background.

**普通话翻译**

大标题的阴影效果看起来有点老旧，不像现在专业服务网站的视觉质感。

**对客户的影响**

屋顶修复是高客单价服务，客户会把网站质感和施工质量联系起来；页面显旧会降低信任，导致更多人不愿意打电话。

**正确长啥样**

A sharper hero with clean white or charcoal type, controlled contrast overlay, 48-64px desktop headline, and no heavy text shadow.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Replace the heavy text-shadow treatment with a darker image overlay or solid content panel area, then set the headline in a clean bold font with tighter spacing.


### 主要 · Quote form is visually crowded

**技术事实**

The desktop quote form sits on the right in a semi-transparent dark block with many checkboxes, radio buttons, yellow labels, and a large text box all visible at once.

**普通话翻译**

报价表单一次问太多问题，看起来很费时间，客户还没建立信任就被要求填写很多内容。

**对客户的影响**

表单字段越多，放弃率通常越高；手机和本地搜索客户尤其想快速联系，复杂表单会损失原本可以转成电话或询价的流量。

**正确长啥样**

A shorter quote form with name, phone, suburb, and one service dropdown first, plus optional details after submission or in a second step.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Redesign the hero form as a compact four-field form with one primary red 'Get free quote' button, moving roof age, scope, and work type into a follow-up step.
