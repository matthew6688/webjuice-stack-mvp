FAQ Sample · editable

Honest answers to the questions homeowners actually ask.

If yours isn't here, call us — we'll tell you straight whether it's worth a visit.

Do you handle small repair jobs?

Yes. Many companies only want to restore entire roofs. We'll take on smaller jobs — a few cracked tiles, a valley leak, a loose ridge cap. If you're not sure whether your job is too small, call us anyway.

How quickly can you come out for a quote?

Usually within 2-3 business days. For urgent leaks or storm damage, we can come out same-day or next-day depending on schedule. Phone is fastest: (07) 3132 1605.

What does a roof restoration cost in Brisbane?

A standard 3-bedroom home is typically in the range of several thousand dollars depending on tile condition and access. We give a fixed written price after the inspection — never an estimate over the phone.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — full public liability insurance and workers compensation. We'll send copies before you sign anything.

Can you work with my insurance company?

For storm damage and accidental events, yes — we provide the photo report, itemised quote, and scope-of-works your insurer needs.
