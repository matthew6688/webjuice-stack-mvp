FAQ Sample · editable

Honest answers to the questions homeowners actually ask.

If yours isn't here, call us — we'll tell you straight whether it's worth a visit.

Restoration or full replacement — how do I know which I need?

Roof age matters less than condition. A 40-year tile roof with sound structure is usually a restoration job. Concrete tiles that have softened, or Colorbond with through-rust, normally need replacement. The inspection report tells you which camp you're in, with photos.

How much does a roof restoration cost in Brisbane?

Every roof is different — age, material, access, and condition all affect the price. We give a fixed written quote after the free inspection, never an estimate over the phone. Most residential restorations take one to two weeks.

I think I have a leak — can you come this week?

Yes. We prioritise leak and storm damage call-outs. Phone is the fastest way: 0410 607 076. We'll get to you within days, sometimes sooner for urgent cases.

Why do your Google reviews mention you refused payment?

If the job is genuinely minor — like a single loose tile that takes five minutes to fix — we won't charge. We'd rather earn the referral. But this doesn't happen on full restoration or repair jobs, obviously.

Can you work directly with my insurance company?

For storm damage and accidental events, yes — we provide the photo report, itemised quote, and scope-of-works your insurer needs.
