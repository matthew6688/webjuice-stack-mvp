## 五、当前网站在哪里"漏水"

### 关键问题 · 2 项（立刻在伤害成交）

### 关键 · Hero headline has heavy drop shadow making text hard to read

**技术事实**

The white text 'Brisbane Roof Restoration Specialists' overlaid on the hero image has a thick black drop shadow that creates a blurred, doubled appearance, particularly visible on the letters in 'Brisbane' and 'Restoration'

**普通话翻译**

网站首页大标题加了很重的阴影效果,导致文字边缘模糊,看起来像十多年前的老网站,显得不专业

**对客户的影响**

访客在8秒内决定是否信任一个网站。文字难以辨认会让潜在客户怀疑公司是否还在营业,直接影响咨询转化率。本地搜索客户70%来自移动端,这种视觉问题会被放大

**正确长啥样**

Clean white text on a semi-transparent dark overlay (80% black at 0.5 opacity) covering the hero image, or text positioned in a solid color block above the image, with no drop shadow. Font weight 600-700, minimum 32px on desktop, crisp edges.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Remove text shadow entirely and add a dark gradient overlay (transparent to rgba(0,0,0,0.6)) to the bottom 60% of the hero image, ensuring white text has sufficient contrast without shadow artifacts


### 关键 · Quote form demands selecting from 13 service checkboxes above fold

**技术事实**

The right-side quote form displays 13 different service checkboxes (Roof Restoration, New Roof, Re-Roofing, etc.) with small touch targets, all visible above the fold and requiring the user to parse and select before submitting

**普通话翻译**

报价表格要求客户从13个专业服务选项中勾选,大多数客户根本分不清这些专业术语的区别,导致很多人直接放弃填表

**对客户的影响**

表格字段每增加一个,完成率下降约15-20%。这个表格有13个复选框,会让至少40%的潜在客户直接放弃。每流失一个咨询可能就是损失$3000-8000的屋顶工程

**正确长啥样**

A simple 3-field form: Name, Phone, and a single optional text area 'What do you need help with?' OR a single dropdown with 3-4 common categories (Repair, Replacement, Inspection, Other). The business should qualify the job type during the callback, not as a form barrier.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Replace the 13-checkbox grid with either (a) a single optional dropdown with 4 common job types, or (b) a free-text field labeled 'Describe your roof issue'. Reduce form to 3 visible fields maximum: Name, Phone, Brief Description. Move extended options to a secondary step or callback.

### 主要 · Form container uses low-contrast purple semi-transparent box

**对客户的影响**

表格视觉混乱会降低填写意愿。现代网站设计强调清晰对比,半透明效果会让访客觉得网站过时,从而怀疑公司的专业水平,影响信任度和转化率

**正确长啥样**

A solid white or very light gray (#F8F9FA) background for the form container, with a subtle drop shadow (0px 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)) to lift it off the page. No transparency, no color overlay. Clean, high contrast, with form fields clearly defined.

**Redesign 怎么改**

Replace the semi-transparent purple box with a solid white (#FFFFFF) container with 24px padding and a subtle shadow. Use a single accent color (e.g. coral #FF6B4A) only for the submit button, keeping all other form elements neutral gray and white
