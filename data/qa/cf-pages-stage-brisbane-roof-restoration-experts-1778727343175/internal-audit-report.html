CRITICAL hero_text_shadow_illegibility
Hero headline has heavy drop shadow making text hard to read

观察到The white text 'Brisbane Roof Restoration Specialists' overlaid on the hero image has a thick black drop shadow that creates a blurred, doubled appearance, particularly visible on the letters in 'Brisbane' and 'Restoration'

为什么是问题A visitor arriving from Google search needs to instantly confirm they're on the right business page. When the business name is difficult to read due to shadow blur, it creates immediate friction and looks unprofessional — like a website made in 2010 using basic text effects. This triggers doubt about whether the business is current and active.

正确长啥样Clean white text on a semi-transparent dark overlay (80% black at 0.5 opacity) covering the hero image, or text positioned in a solid color block above the image, with no drop shadow. Font weight 600-700, minimum 32px on desktop, crisp edges.

redesign 怎么改Remove text shadow entirely and add a dark gradient overlay (transparent to rgba(0,0,0,0.6)) to the bottom 60% of the hero image, ensuring white text has sufficient contrast without shadow artifacts

CRITICAL desktop_13_service_checkboxes
Quote form demands selecting from 13 service checkboxes above fold

观察到The right-side quote form displays 13 different service checkboxes (Roof Restoration, New Roof, Re-Roofing, etc.) with small touch targets, all visible above the fold and requiring the user to parse and select before submitting

为什么是问题A homeowner searching 'roofer Brisbane' typically knows they need 'roof repair' but doesn't know industry terminology like 're-roofing' vs 'roof restoration' vs 're-bedding'. Forcing them to choose from 13 technical options creates decision paralysis and form abandonment. Forms with more than 3-4 fields see 30-40% lower completion rates. This looks like a filter for the business's convenience, not the customer's.

正确长啥样A simple 3-field form: Name, Phone, and a single optional text area 'What do you need help with?' OR a single dropdown with 3-4 common categories (Repair, Replacement, Inspection, Other). The business should qualify the job type during the callback, not as a form barrier.

redesign 怎么改Replace the 13-checkbox grid with either (a) a single optional dropdown with 4 common job types, or (b) a free-text field labeled 'Describe your roof issue'. Reduce form to 3 visible fields maximum: Name, Phone, Brief Description. Move extended options to a secondary step or callback.

MAJOR desktop_no_trust_signals_above_fold
No reviews, credentials, or social proof visible above fold

观察到Above the fold, the only content is the hero image, headline with shadow, and the 13-checkbox form. There are no Google review stars, no '4.8★ (127 reviews)' badge, no years in business, no licenses or certifications, no before/after photos — nothing that proves this is a real, established, quality business

为什么是问题84% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations. A visitor from Google has just seen this business listed with competitors — they need immediate proof of credibility. When the first screen shows only a form and generic headline, it looks like a lead-generation funnel, not a real local business with a track record. This is especially critical for high-ticket home services ($5k-15k roof jobs) where trust determines conversion.

正确长啥样Within the first viewport: a Google review badge (e.g. '4.9★★★★★ from 94 Google reviews'), '15+ Years Serving Brisbane', a license number or industry association logo (MBA, HIA), and ideally a small before/after image pair or a customer photo testimonial

redesign 怎么改Add a trust bar directly below the hero headline showing: Google star rating + review count, years in business, and 1-2 credential badges (QBCC license, insurance). Place a 2-photo before/after thumbnail in the hero area or immediately below the CTA button

MAJOR desktop_red_cta_button_too_small
Primary phone CTA button is undersized and low contrast

观察到The red 'Call (07) 3184 1606' button in the left hero area is approximately 180px wide and 36px tall, with thin text that doesn't stand out strongly against the shadowed hero background

为什么是问题On desktop, a primary call button should be immediately visible and clickable without precision. This button is small enough to look like secondary navigation rather than the main conversion action. For a local service business where 60-70% of conversions happen by phone, the call button should be the most prominent element on the page. Small buttons signal low confidence or look like ads, reducing click-through.

正确长啥样A primary CTA button at least 200px wide × 50px tall on desktop, high contrast (e.g. solid coral #FF6B4A on white, or white on dark), with 16-18px bold text, positioned in the top-right header OR as a large hero button. The phone number should be visible as plain text separately, with the button saying 'Call Now' or 'Get Free Quote'

redesign 怎么改Increase the red CTA button to minimum 220px × 52px, use bolder font weight (700), and ensure it has a solid background with no transparency. Consider duplicating the call button in the sticky header and as a large hero CTA below the headline

MAJOR desktop_form_purple_box_low_contrast
Form container uses low-contrast purple semi-transparent box

观察到The quote form on the right is contained within a purple/blue semi-transparent box that overlays the hero image, making the background texture and house photo partially visible through the form background

为什么是问题Forms need high contrast and clear boundaries to feel trustworthy and easy to complete. A semi-transparent colored overlay creates visual noise — the user's eye has to work to separate form fields from the background pattern. This looks decorative rather than functional, and in 2025, decorative gradients and transparency are associated with outdated web design (2012-2015 era). Low contrast also reduces perceived professionalism.

正确长啥样A solid white or very light gray (#F8F9FA) background for the form container, with a subtle drop shadow (0px 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)) to lift it off the page. No transparency, no color overlay. Clean, high contrast, with form fields clearly defined.

redesign 怎么改Replace the semi-transparent purple box with a solid white (#FFFFFF) container with 24px padding and a subtle shadow. Use a single accent color (e.g. coral #FF6B4A) only for the submit button, keeping all other form elements neutral gray and white

MINOR dated_logo_simple_icon
Logo uses simple house icon with thin text lacking visual weight

观察到The top-left logo consists of a basic red house outline icon next to 'BRISBANE ROOF RESTORATION EXPERTS' in thin, condensed uppercase text, with significant letter spacing

为什么是问题Thin typography and generic icon-based logos were common in 2010-2015 but now signal a template or DIY website rather than an established business. While not a conversion blocker, it subtly undermines brand authority — especially when competing against businesses with bold, confident branding. First impressions form in 50 milliseconds, and logo design is part of that snap judgment.

正确长啥样Either (a) a wordmark logo with medium-to-bold weight typography (font-weight 600-700), or (b) a custom icon that reflects the specific business (e.g. a stylized roof shape, not a generic house). Modern service business logos favor bold, readable text over thin condensed fonts.

redesign 怎么改Redesign the logo with a bolder, more confident typeface (semi-bold sans-serif) and either remove the house icon or replace it with a custom roof-specific mark. Ensure the logo is legible at small sizes on mobile
