MAJOR dated_logo_and_header
Dated logo weakens first impression

观察到The header logo uses a thin black roof outline, red uppercase 'BRISBANE' text, and small underlined 'roof restoration experts' text that looks like an old clip-art style mark.

为什么是问题A Google-searching local customer often judges whether a trades business is real and current within seconds. This logo makes the business look older and less established, even before the visitor reads the page.

正确长啥样A crisp wordmark with one strong roof icon, heavier readable typography, and consistent red/charcoal branding that still works clearly on mobile.

redesign 怎么改Replace the existing logo treatment with a simplified roofing wordmark, remove the underline, use a professional sans-serif typeface, and size it consistently across desktop and mobile headers.

证据截图 · Full desktop

MAJOR busy_hero_with_heavy_shadow_text
Hero image makes text feel dated

观察到The hero section uses a full-width house photo with a dark overlay, large white headline text, and heavy grey drop shadows behind the words.

为什么是问题The shadowed text and darkened image feel older and less polished. A homeowner looking for roof work may read this as a low-budget website and become less confident about calling.

正确长啥样A brighter hero with a high-quality roof restoration image, clean white or dark text without heavy shadows, and a clear headline area separated from the busy parts of the photo.

redesign 怎么改Use a sharper roofing project photo, apply a subtle flat overlay only where text sits, remove the thick text shadow, and set the headline in a cleaner 44-56px desktop type scale with tighter line spacing.

证据截图 · Desktop 700px region

CRITICAL quote_form_too_demanding_above_fold
Quote form asks too much too soon

观察到The desktop hero form asks for name, phone, email, location, roof type, scope of work, work type, roof age, and a description before the red 'REQUEST QUOTE' button.

为什么是问题A cold visitor from Google may not be ready to answer that many questions immediately. The form feels like work, so some people will choose an easier competitor or just call later and forget.

正确长啥样A short first-step form with name, phone, suburb, and service need, plus an optional details field after the visitor starts the quote process.

redesign 怎么改Convert the hero form into a 4-field quick quote form: name, phone, suburb, service type. Move roof age, scope, and extra details into a second step or follow-up call.

MAJOR mobile_quote_path_hidden
Mobile quote form is not visible

观察到On the mobile screenshot, the visible first screen shows the logo, hamburger icon, search icon, headline, supporting text, and red phone button, but no quote form or 'request quote' option.

为什么是问题Many local roofing searches happen on phones. If a visitor wants a written quote instead of calling immediately, they have no visible path in the first screen and may leave.

正确长啥样Mobile first screen with the phone button and a second 'Get quote' button stacked below it, both visible without scrolling.

redesign 怎么改Add a secondary mobile CTA directly under the red call button: 'Get Free Quote'. Link it to a short mobile-optimized quote form placed immediately below the hero.

MAJOR generic_trust_signals_missing
No proof visible above fold

观察到The visible desktop and mobile hero areas do not show review stars, years in business, licence details, warranty wording, before-and-after work, or Brisbane suburb proof.

为什么是问题Roofing is a high-trust purchase. A local customer comparing several Brisbane roofers wants quick proof that the business is reputable before sharing contact details.

正确长啥样A compact trust row under the headline or CTA showing Google rating, years of experience, warranty, insured/licensed wording, and Brisbane service area.

redesign 怎么改Add a trust strip above the fold with review stars, 'Brisbane owned', warranty/insurance claim, and one short line about local roof restoration experience.

MINOR desktop_form_visual_clutter
Form area feels visually crowded

观察到The right side of the desktop hero has many white input boxes, radio buttons, yellow section labels, and fine-print legal links placed over the house photo.

为什么是问题The busy form competes with the hero message and makes the page feel harder to scan. A visitor may hesitate because the action looks more complicated than it needs to be.

正确长啥样A quiet white form panel with clear spacing, one label style, larger touch targets, and only essential fields visible.
