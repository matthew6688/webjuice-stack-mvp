Keep this front and centre — it's your best differentiator.

What's Holding You Back

Browsers warn visitors your site is "Not Secure"
Your website doesn't have HTTPS security. This means every visitor sees a "Not Secure" warning in their browser's address bar. Some browsers even show a full-screen warning before letting people view your site.
Google has ranked secure sites higher since 2018. Research shows that over 80% of visitors will close a "Not Secure" site immediately and move on to the next company. With zero Google reviews to fall back on, you can't afford to lose visitors before they even read a word about your service.
Your website looks like it hasn't been updated since 2012
The gradient navigation bar, embossed logo, and overall design all signal an unmaintained, outdated business. Modern websites use flat, clean designs — yours uses effects that were popular over a decade ago.
Visitors judge trustworthiness in about 8 seconds. An outdated design leads roughly 40% of first-time visitors to close the page and click on the next roofing company in Google's results — even if your service is better and your prices are more competitive.
Your quote form asks for too much information up front
Your contact form demands ten fields including full street address, email, phone, name, service type, and a detailed description — all before someone has even decided to trust you.
Research shows that every extra form field reduces completion rates by about 11%. A ten-field form compared to a two-field form can lose you over 50% of potential inquiries. That's dozens of lost quote requests every month — people who wanted to contact you but gave up because it felt like too much work.
There's no proof other customers trust you
Your Google Business Profile shows 4.5 stars but zero reviews, and there's no review badge, star rating, or customer testimonial anywhere on your homepage. New visitors have no quick way to verify you're trustworthy.
Over 90% of consumers check online reviews before booking a local service. Displaying Google reviews on your homepage can increase inquiries by 20–30% because visitors don't need to leave your site to verify your reputation — reducing the chance they'll wander off to a competitor.
Visitors can't tell what you do within 3 seconds
Your homepage hero area is split 50/50: one side shows a worker, the other shows a house — but there's no large headline text anywhere to confirm what service you offer or where you work.
Most people spend less than 15 seconds on a new webpage. Without a clear headline, many will leave simply because they can't quickly confirm you're the right fit. A clear, prominent headline typically increases visitor engagement by about 30%.
