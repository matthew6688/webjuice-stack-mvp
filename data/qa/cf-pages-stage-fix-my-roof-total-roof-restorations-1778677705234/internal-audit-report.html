CRITICAL cookie_banner_blocking
Cookie consent banner covers 40% of viewport on load

观察到A black rounded rectangle cookie banner in the bottom-left corner covers approximately 40% of the visible content, with white text and two buttons ('Reject' and 'Accept') plus a 'Learn more and customise' link beneath

为什么是问题A visitor searching 'roofing Brisbane' lands on this page and immediately sees a large dark box blocking the business description and key information. Within the 8-second decision window, they cannot read what services are offered or see contact details without first dismissing an obstruction. This creates friction at the exact moment trust needs to be established.

正确长啥样A slim single-line banner at the very top or bottom (max 60px height) with inline 'Accept' link, or a centered modal overlay with clear close button that doesn't obscure the hero content. Modern GDPR banners use 5-10% of screen height, not 40%.

redesign 怎么改Remove intrusive cookie banner entirely from the custom redesign; if legally required, implement a 50px bottom-strip banner with inline accept/decline buttons that doesn't obstruct business name, hero image, or primary CTA

MAJOR purple_yellow_brand_clash
Purple and bright yellow color scheme feels generic/dated

观察到The header uses a deep purple background (#5B3A9D approximate) with a bright yellow/gold house logo and yellow accent on 'Total Roof Restorations' tagline below the white 'FIX MY ROOF' text

为什么是问题Purple-and-yellow is a high-contrast combination commonly seen in 2010-era small business branding (think Aussie sports clubs or budget services). For a premium roofing restoration business targeting Brisbane homeowners, this palette doesn't convey the trust and craftsmanship expected. Local searchers comparing multiple roofers will perceive this as less established than competitors using navy, charcoal, or earth tones.

正确长啥样A professional palette: navy or charcoal primary, coral or warm orange accent for CTAs, with white space and minimal use of accent color. Logo should use 1-2 colors maximum. Think Dulux or James Hardie branding: understated, trustworthy, premium.

redesign 怎么改Redesign brand palette: replace purple with navy (#1A3A52), replace bright yellow with coral (#FF6B47) for CTA buttons only, keep white text on navy background for header, use neutral grey (#F5F5F5) for page background, limit accent color to CTAs and key highlights

MINOR text_wall_description

正确长啥样Services should be presented as scannable elements: either a bulleted list with icons (✓ Roof restoration • ✓ Pressure washing • ✓ Re-pointing) or three visual cards with a small icon, service name, and 1-line description. redesign 怎么改Break the text wall into three sections: (1) short intro sentence 'We specialize in roof restoration for Brisbane Qld homes and businesses', (2) bulleted service list with checkmark icons for each service (pressure washing, re-pointing, re-bedging, etc.), (3) separate testimonial card below with quoted text and 5-star visual; use 18px line height and ample white space
