The site has basic conversion elements in place but suffers from low-trust stock photography, unclear visual hierarchy, and dated design patterns that undermine credibility for a premium local service.

CRITICAL generic_hero_stock_photo
Hero image shows family in hardhats instead of actual work

观察到The hero section displays a stock photo of adults and children wearing safety equipment in what appears to be a staged domestic scene, not actual roofing work or completed projects

为什么是问题When someone searches 'gutter repair Brisbane' they want proof this business does real work. A generic stock photo with children in hardhats signals the business can't show their actual projects, making visitors question if they're legitimate or just reselling leads. Real local customers immediately bounce when they can't see evidence of actual work.

正确长啥样Hero image showing a before/after of an actual Brisbane roof restoration, or a real crew member on a local job site with visible branding on the work vehicle. Background should show recognizable Brisbane architecture or suburbs to anchor local credibility.

redesign 怎么改Replace with authentic project photography from actual completed jobs in Brisbane suburbs, showing real crew, branded vehicles, or dramatic before/after transformations. If no photos exist, commission a one-day photoshoot of an actual job.

MAJOR phone_number_visibility
Phone number in header is small and low-contrast

观察到The phone number '0422 052 592' appears in the top-left header in small dark text against the background, with minimal visual weight compared to the 'Book Now' button on the opposite side

为什么是问题For emergency gutter or roof repairs, many Brisbane customers want to call immediately, especially older demographics or urgent situations (storm damage, leaks). If they have to hunt for the phone number, they'll hit back and call the next search result where the number is obvious. Mobile users especially won't squint at small header text.

正确长啥样Phone number displayed prominently in the top-right corner in large, high-contrast text (at least 18px), possibly with a phone icon, and making it a tap-to-call link on mobile. It should have equal or greater visual weight than any form CTA.

redesign 怎么改Increase phone number size to 20px+, move to top-right header with high contrast (dark on light or vice versa), add phone icon, ensure it's a clickable tel: link. Consider duplicating it above the fold in the hero area for mobile users.

MAJOR form_field_overload
Quote form has 7 fields including optional ones

观察到The 'Request a Quick Quote' form on the right side of the hero contains 7 input fields: Name, Phone, Email, Street Address, Suburb, Post Code, and 'Tell us about it' text area, all above the fold

为什么是问题Local search visitors are comparison shopping across 3-5 businesses. Each additional form field exponentially increases friction. Research shows forms with more than 3 fields see 20-40% drop in completion rates. Asking for street address and postcode separately (when postcode alone works for quote estimation) signals bureaucracy, not speed.

正确长啥样Mobile-first form with maximum 3 fields: phone number (primary), suburb or postcode (for service area verification), and optional message. Name and email can be collected after initial contact. Single large CTA button below fields.

redesign 怎么改Reduce to 3 core fields: Phone (required), Suburb (required for Brisbane service area), Brief description (optional). Remove name, email, and separate address fields. Add trust badge below button ('Usually respond within 2 hours' or '127 Brisbane repairs this month').

MINOR orange_color_inconsistency
Bottom orange navigation bar clashes with blue brand CTA

观察到The bottom navigation bar is bright orange with white icons, while the primary 'Book Now' button in the header and the 'Submit' button in the form are both bright blue, creating competing color schemes

为什么是问题Inconsistent accent colors fragment visual attention and make the site feel cobbled together from different templates. For a premium service where trust matters, this signals lack of professional design oversight. Visitors subconsciously wonder if the business is equally inconsistent in their workmanship.

正确长啥样Single primary accent color (either the blue or orange, not both) used consistently for all CTAs, buttons, and interactive elements. Secondary brand color reserved for backgrounds or supporting elements only.

redesign 怎么改Choose blue as primary CTA color (more trustworthy for service businesses), replace orange navigation bar with white or light gray, use orange sparingly as an accent (hover states, icons). Maintain visual consistency across all interactive elements.

MINOR hero_text_contrast
White hero text over busy photo background lacks readability

观察到The headline 'Brisbane Gutter & Roofing Experts' and body text are white, overlaid directly on the stock photo background where the image has varying brightness levels, making some words harder to read

为什么是问题If a visitor can't instantly read your value proposition in the first 2 seconds, they assume the site isn't relevant and hit back. Poor text contrast especially hurts older demographics (common for homeowners needing roof repairs) and mobile users viewing in bright sunlight.

正确长啥样Hero text placed over a dark semi-transparent overlay (40-60% opacity black or dark blue gradient) that covers the background image, ensuring consistent high contrast. Alternatively, position text in a solid-color sidebar box separate from the image area.

redesign 怎么改Add a dark gradient overlay (top-to-bottom or left-to-right) over the hero image, starting at 70% opacity where text sits, fading to 20% at edges. This maintains image visibility while guaranteeing text readability across all devices and lighting conditions.

MAJOR no_social_proof_above_fold
No reviews, testimonials, or trust badges visible above fold

观察到The hero section contains the form and headline but no visible Google reviews rating, number of completed jobs, years in business, licensing/insurance badges, or customer testimonials

为什么是问题Local service customers are risk-averse because they're inviting strangers to their property for expensive work. Without immediate social proof, they have no reason to trust this business over the 4 other roofing companies in the search results. They'll back out to check reviews elsewhere or call a competitor who displays their 4.8-star rating prominently.

正确长啥样Google reviews widget or star rating (e.g. '4.8 stars from 127 Brisbane customers') positioned directly below or beside the headline. Trust badges (licensed, insured, 15+ years) in a horizontal row below the form or CTA. Optional: One-sentence testimonial quote from a named Brisbane suburb customer.

redesign 怎么改Add Google reviews summary (star count + review count) directly below the headline with a link to the full GBP reviews page. Include trust badge row (QBCC licensed, $20M insured, Family owned since 2008) immediately above or below the form submit button. Pull one strong testimonial to display in a light background box beside the form.
