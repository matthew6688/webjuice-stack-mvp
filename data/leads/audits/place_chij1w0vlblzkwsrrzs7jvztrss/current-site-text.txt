At "À la Bonne Franquette", we embrace the essence of the French citation meaning "simple, good, no fuss." This informal yet charming expression encapsulates our philosophy: to provide food that is uncomplicated yet delicious.

Our restaurant takes pride in offering high-quality, flavourful French cuisine that feels just like home. We aim to delight a diverse clientele with authentic flavours, generous portions, and affordable prices.

At "À la Bonne Franquette," we're on a mission to democratise French cuisine, dispelling the notion that it must always be elaborate or high-end. Join us to savour the hearty, comforting dishes and experience the true essence of French dining.
