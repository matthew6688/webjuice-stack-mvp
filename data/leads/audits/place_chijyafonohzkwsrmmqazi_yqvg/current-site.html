Just a short stroll from the Brisbane River and Powerhouse Theater, Common Vice is your local go-to for expressive Australian natural wines, cold craft beer and Modern Australian, European style share plates.

Wine Pairing Wednesdays

Wednesdays at Common Vice are our weekly celebration of the things we all have in common: a love of great food, good company, and a little midweek vino. Every Wednesday our $45pp set menu includes a rotating selection of entrees, mains and desserts. For an additional $30pp we provide 3 glasses of wine designed to complement your meal which we will bring to the table and share a little about each one. Perfect for a girl dinner catch-up.
