The Balfour Kitchen & Bar

In the heart of New Farm, The Balfour Kitchen & Bar offers a dining experience that transitions seamlessly from relaxed daytime gatherings to intimate evening occasions. Set within a beautifully restored Queenslander, the space blends heritage charm with eclectic, contemporary design, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for everything from casual brunches to cocktail-style events.

Book
Our dining

The Balfour Kitchen & Bar is a destination for those who appreciate thoughtful food, curated wines, and a setting full of personality. By day, it’s a light-filled space for breakfast and brunch; by night, it transforms into a moody wine bar perfect for shared plates and social dining. With multiple spaces including a rooftop bar, it’s ideal for both casual catch-ups and private celebrations.

In the heart of New Farm, The Balfour Kitchen & Bar is a local favourite, offering a dining experience that evolves throughout the day. By morning, it’s a relaxed spot for daily breakfast and weekend brunches, where classic dishes, fresh coffee, and signature brunch cocktails are enjoyed beneath the shade of our leafy plane tree.

Guests of Balfour can choose to dine inside, alfresco on the breezy verandah, or in the leafy courtyard, enjoying the chic, cosy atmosphere with old-school jazz vibes.

The renovated Queenslander building, with its heritage doors, rich textures, and vibrant accents such as purple velvet chairs and Art Deco-patterned couches, created a ‘home away from home’ atmosphere that feels both welcoming and unique.

As evening sets in, the atmosphere shifts effortlessly into a moody, intimate wine bar. The charcoal-clad exterior, dim lighting, and fairy-lit courtyard set the scene for sophisticated share plates, premium charcuterie, artisan cheeses and playful, chef-crafted dishes designed for sharing. The quirky, eclectic design makes it a destination that feels stylish yet unmistakably personal.

For a truly elevated experience, our rooftop bar is available for private events and functions. With views of the Brisbane skyline, it provides a chic and memorable space for celebrations, corporate gatherings, or intimate get-togethers.

Our kitchen champions sustainable seafood, ethically sourced meats, and hero vegetables, all sourced from a carefully hand-picked team of small-scale, quality food suppliers in New Farm. The menu balances familiar comfort with contemporary flair, creating flavourful dishes that are both approachable and sophisticated. From signature brunch specials to share plates such as saku tuna with watermelon and lime or wild kangaroo salami with house pickles, each dish reflects a story of craft and quality.

Complementing the food is a curated selection of French and Australian wines, crisp whites and elegant reds served at their best thanks to our Coravin preservation system. Our wine list showcases small-batch, contemporary Australian producers with a focus on adventurous varietals and expressive winemaking. Expect boutique labels and plenty of personality in every pour, perfect for curious drinkers seeking something different. Classic cocktails, craft spirits, and refreshing non-alcoholic options round out a menu designed to suit every mood.

Guests of Balfour can choose to dine inside, alfresco on the breezy verandah, or in the leafy courtyard, enjoying the chic, cosy atmosphere with old-school jazz vibes.
