Not just a place, but a feeling — comfort, connection, belonging.

Inspired by tropical regions and island heritage, Ruma is built on the idea that the best moments happen around a table.

Set in the heart of New Farm, Ruma is a rooftop escape shaped by tropical warmth and effortless style.

Elevated above little lane, the space blends lush textures, open air, and vibrant energy with quality-led dining and considered cocktails, creating a setting that feels relaxed, transportive, and alive.

HELMED BY CHEF FELIPE OLIVEIRA,

RUMA’S KITCHEN IS DRIVEN BY QUALITY AND INTENT — WITH THE CHEF’S TABLE EXPERIENCE OFFERING A FRONT-ROW VIEW INTO THE SOUL OF THE MENU, PRESENTED IN ITS MOST CONSIDERED EXPRESSION.
