About us

Dicki’s New Farm is a welcoming plant-based café nestled on Brunswick Street.

 

Offering both indoor and al fresco dining choices, guests can also enjoy a quaint balcony area upfront. For those on-the-go, there’s a convenient takeaway bench inside.

 

At Dicki’s we pride ourselves on doing fabulous coffee and have a wide range of alternative milks. We have a great smoothie selection, an all day menu, and we are dog friendly as we love all our furry friends!
